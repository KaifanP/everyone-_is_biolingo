# 第28单元 间接引语进阶（转述疑问句和祈使句）

## 学习目标
1. 掌握疑问句的间接引语转换
2. 掌握祈使句的间接引语转换
3. 理解不同引导词的选择
4. 能够正确转述各种类型的句子

## 一、导入 (Lead-in)

### 思考问题
- 转述疑问句时还需要用问号吗？
- 转述祈使句时用什么动词？

### 对比观察
- **直接引语：** He asked, "Where do you live?"
- **间接引语：** He asked where I lived.（注意：用陈述句语序）

- **直接引语：** She said, "Close the door."
- **间接引语：** She told me to close the door.

## 二、讲解 (Presentation)

### 2.1 一般疑问句的间接引语

**结构：主语 + asked/wondered + if/whether + 陈述句语序**

| 直接引语 | 间接引语 |
|---------|---------|
| "Are you a student?" | He asked if/whether I was a student. |
| "Do you like coffee?" | She asked if/whether I liked coffee. |
| "Did you go there?" | They asked if/whether I had gone there. |

**注意：** if和whether可以互换，但whether更正式

### 2.2 特殊疑问句的间接引语

**结构：主语 + asked + 疑问词 + 陈述句语序**

| 直接引语 | 间接引语 |
|---------|---------|
| "Where do you live?" | He asked where I lived. |
| "What is your name?" | She asked what my name was. |
| "When did you arrive?" | They asked when I had arrived. |
| "Why are you late?" | He asked why I was late. |
| "How old are you?" | She asked how old I was. |

**关键：** 疑问词不变，语序变为陈述句语序

### 2.3 祈使句的间接引语

**肯定祈使句：tell/ask/order/advise + sb + to do**

| 直接引语 | 间接引语 |
|---------|---------|
| "Open the door." | He told me to open the door. |
| "Please sit down." | She asked me to sit down. |
| "Don't be late." | He told me not to be late. |
| "Stop talking." | The teacher ordered us to stop talking. |

**否定祈使句：tell/ask + sb + not + to do**

| 直接引语 | 间接引语 |
|---------|---------|
| "Don't go." | He told me not to go. |
| "Please don't smoke." | She asked me not to smoke. |

**常用转述动词：**
| 动词 | 语气 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| tell | 一般 | told me to go |
| ask | 礼貌 | asked me to go |
| order | 命令 | ordered me to go |
| advise | 建议 | advised me to go |
| warn | 警告 | warned me not to go |
| remind | 提醒 | reminded me to go |

### 2.4 感叹句的间接引语

| 直接引语 | 间接引语 |
|---------|---------|
| "What a beautiful day!" | He said it was a beautiful day. |
| "How wonderful!" | She said it was wonderful. |

## 三、辨析 (Analysis)

### 常见错误

1. ❌ He asked where did I live.
   ✅ He asked where I lived.
   **分析：** 间接引语中用陈述句语序，不用倒装

2. ❌ She told to me to open the door.
   ✅ She told me to open the door.
   **分析：** tell后面直接接人，不需要to

3. ❌ He asked me that I was a student.
   ✅ He asked me if/whether I was a student.
   **分析：** 疑问句用if/whether，不用that

### 关键辨析

**疑问句类型判断**
| 直接引语特征 | 间接引语引导词 |
|-------------|---------------|
| 以be/do/have开头的一般疑问句 | if / whether |
| 以what/where/when等开头 | 对应的疑问词 |
| 选择疑问句 | whether...or |

## 四、练习 (Practice)

### 练习1：一般疑问句转述
将下列句子改为间接引语：
1. "Are you a teacher?" she asked.
2. "Do you like music?" he asked.
3. "Have you been to Paris?" they asked.

### 练习2：特殊疑问句转述
1. "Where is the station?" she asked.
2. "What time does the train leave?" he asked.
3. "Why didn't you come?" they asked.

### 练习3：祈使句转述
1. "Please help me," she said.
2. "Don't touch it," the teacher said.
3. "Be careful!" he warned.

## 五、产出 (Production)

### 任务1：采访转述
假设你采访了一位名人，写出5个你问的问题以及他/她的回答，然后全部转述为间接引语。

### 任务2：指令转述
将以下场景转述为间接引语：
- 医生对病人说："每天吃三次药，多喝水。"
- 老师对学生说："不要迟到，明天交作业。"

## 六、总结 (Summary)

### 关键要点
1. 一般疑问句 → asked + if/whether + 陈述句语序
2. 特殊疑问句 → asked + 疑问词 + 陈述句语序
3. 祈使句 → told/asked + sb + (not) + to do
4. 间接引语中不用倒装语序

### 自测题
1. She asked me ______ I liked coffee.
   A. that B. if C. what D. which

2. He told me ______ quiet.
   A. be B. to be C. being D. was

3. "Where do you work?" she asked.
   → She asked where ______.
   A. did I work B. I worked C. do I work D. I work

**答案：1.B 2.B 3.B**
