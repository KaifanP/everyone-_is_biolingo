# 第29单元 被动语态与间接引语综合实战

## 学习目标
1. 综合运用被动语态和间接引语
2. 在实际语境中灵活使用这两种语法结构
3. 掌握考试中常见的被动语态和间接引语考点
4. 提升阅读和写作中的语法准确性

## 一、导入 (Lead-in)

### 思考问题
- 新闻报道为什么大量使用被动语态？
- 间接引语在学术写作中有什么作用？

### 真实语境示例
**新闻报道（被动语态）：**
> The president was elected last month. The new policy has been announced. The decision was made by the committee.

**学术论文（间接引语）：**
> Smith (2020) argued that the results were significant. He suggested that further research should be conducted.

## 二、讲解 (Presentation)

### 2.1 被动语态 + 间接引语的组合

**常见场景：**
1. **新闻报道**
   - It was announced that the president would resign.
   - The president announced that he would resign.

2. **学术写作**
   - It is believed that the theory is correct.
   - Scientists believe that the theory is correct.

3. **正式通知**
   - It has been decided that the meeting will be postponed.
   - The manager decided that the meeting would be postponed.

### 2.2 It is said/believed/reported that... 结构

**常用句型：**
| 主动 | 被动 |
|------|------|
| People say that... | It is said that... |
| People believe that... | It is believed that... |
| People think that... | It is thought that... |
| People report that... | It is reported that... |
| People know that... | It is known that... |

**例句：**
- It is said that he is a genius.
- It is reported that the accident was serious.
- It is believed that the plan will succeed.

### 2.3 被动语态在不同语境中的应用

**科技论文：**
- The experiment was conducted by the research team.
- The data were analyzed using statistical methods.

**法律文件：**
- The contract was signed by both parties.
- The decision was made by the court.

**日常对话：**
- My phone was stolen yesterday.
- The meeting has been canceled.

## 三、辨析 (Analysis)

### 综合错误分析

1. ❌ It is said that he was a genius.
   ✅ It is said that he is a genius.
   **分析：** 如果转述的内容仍然是事实，时态不需要后退

2. ❌ The president was said to resign.
   ✅ The president was said to be going to resign.
   **分析：** 不定式结构需要完整

3. ❌ He said that the letter was written by him.
   ✅ He said that he had written the letter.
   **分析：** 间接引语中通常用主动语态，除非特别强调被动

### 考试常见考点

1. **时态一致性**
   - 主句过去时，从句通常也用过去相关时态
   - 例外：客观真理用一般现在时

2. **被动语态的时态**
   - 注意be动词的时态变化
   - 完成时被动 = have/has been + 过去分词

3. **间接引语的语序**
   - 疑问句转述后用陈述句语序

## 四、练习 (Practice)

### 练习1：综合改写
将下列句子改写为被动语态和间接引语：
1. People say that Shakespeare wrote Hamlet.
2. The teacher told us, "The exam will be held next week."
3. Someone has stolen the painting. The police reported this.

### 练习2：新闻报道改写
将以下新闻改写为被动语态和间接引语：
- The CEO said, "We will launch the new product next month."
- Scientists have discovered a new species. This was announced yesterday.

### 练习3：错误改正
找出并改正下列句子中的错误：
1. It is said that the earth was round.
2. He told that he would come.
3. The book was wrote by a famous author.

## 五、产出 (Production)

### 任务1：新闻写作
用被动语态和间接引语写一篇简短的新闻报道（5-6句），内容自选。

### 任务2：学术段落改写
将以下段落改写为更正式的学术风格（使用被动语态和间接引语）：
> I think this method works well. My team tested it last year. We found that it improved efficiency by 30%.

## 六、总结 (Summary)

### 关键要点
1. 被动语态和间接引语常组合使用，尤其在新闻和学术写作中
2. "It is said that..." 结构是被动语态和间接引语的结合
3. 注意时态一致性和语序变化
4. 在正式写作中优先使用被动语态和间接引语

### 知识图谱
```
被动语态与间接引语
├── 被动语态
│   ├── 基本结构：be + 过去分词
│   ├── 各时态变化
│   └── 使用场景
├── 间接引语
│   ├── 陈述句：said/told + that
│   ├── 疑问句：asked + if/whether/wh-
│   └── 祈使句：told/asked + to do
└── 综合运用
    ├── 新闻报道
    ├── 学术写作
    └── 正式通知
```

   A. reports B. is reported C. reported D. is reporting

2. She said that she ______ the report by Friday.
   A. will finish B. would finish C. finishes D. finished

3. The new law ______ by the parliament last week.
   A. was passed B. passed C. has passed D. is passed

**答案：1.B 2.B 3.A**
