# 第27单元 间接引语基础（转述陈述句）

## 学习目标
1. 理解直接引语与间接引语的区别
2. 掌握人称和时态的变化规则
3. 掌握时间状语和地点状语的变化
4. 能够将直接引语转述为间接引语

## 一、导入 (Lead-in)

### 思考问题
- 为什么转述别人的话时需要改变时态？
- 如果不改变时态会有什么问题？

### 对比观察
- **直接引语：** Tom said, "I am busy."
- **间接引语：** Tom said (that) he was busy.

**变化：** 引号消失，人称I→he，时态am→was

## 二、讲解 (Presentation)

### 2.1 直接引语 vs 间接引语
| 类型 | 特征 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 直接引语 | 保留原话，加引号 | He said, "I like it." |
| 间接引语 | 转述内容，不加引号 | He said (that) he liked it. |

### 2.2 人称变化规则
**原则：根据转述者的视角调整**
- 直接：He said, "I love my job."
- 间接：He said (that) he loved his job.

- 直接：She said, "You are wrong."
- 间接：She said (that) I was wrong.

### 2.3 时态变化规则（后退一步）

| 直接引语 | 间接引语 |
|---------|---------|
| 一般现在时 | 一般过去时 |
| 现在进行时 | 过去进行时 |
| 一般过去时 | 过去完成时 |
| 现在完成时 | 过去完成时 |
| 过去完成时 | 过去完成时（不变） |
| will | would |
| can | could |
| may | might |
| shall | should |
| must | had to / must |

**例句：**
- "I am happy." → He said (that) he was happy.
- "I have finished." → He said (that) he had finished.
- "I will come." → He said (that) he would come.
- "I can swim." → He said (that) he could swim.

### 2.4 时间和地点状语变化

| 直接引语 | 间接引语 |
|---------|---------|
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| yesterday | the day before |
| tomorrow | the next day / the following day |
| this week | that week |
| last week | the week before |
| next week | the following week |
| here | there |
| this | that |
| these | those |

## 三、辨析 (Analysis)

### 常见错误

1. ❌ He said that he is happy.
   ✅ He said that he was happy.
   **分析：** 间接引语中时态需要后退

2. ❌ She said me that she was tired.
   ✅ She told me that she was tired.
   ✅ She said that she was tired.
   **分析：** say后面不接人，tell后面必须接人

3. ❌ He said he has finished yesterday.
   ✅ He said he had finished the day before.
   **分析：** 时态和时间状语都需要变化

### 关键辨析

**say vs tell**
| 动词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| say | say (that)... / say to sb | He said that... |
| tell | tell sb that... | He told me that... |

## 四、练习 (Practice)

### 练习1：时态变化
将直接引语改为间接引语：
1. She said, "I am cooking dinner."
2. He said, "I have seen the movie."
3. They said, "We will help you."

### 练习2：时间和地点变化
1. She said, "I will come here tomorrow."
2. He said, "I saw this yesterday."
3. They said, "We are busy now."

### 练习3：综合改写
将下列对话改为间接引语：
Tom said, "I am studying English now. I have studied for two hours. I will finish soon."

## 五、产出 (Production)

### 任务1：转述新闻
将以下新闻标题改写为间接引语：
- The president said, "We will reduce taxes."
- The scientist said, "Climate change is accelerating."

### 任务2：转述对话
转述你和朋友最近的一段对话（至少3个回合）。

## 六、总结 (Summary)

### 关键要点
1. 间接引语 = 去引号 + 人称变化 + 时态后退
2. 时间和地点状语也需要相应变化
3. say后面不接人，tell后面必须接人
4. 时态后退是核心规则

### 自测题
1. He said he ______ busy at that moment.
   A. is B. was C. has been D. will be

2. She told me that she ______ the next day.
   A. will come B. would come C. comes D. came

3. "I have finished my homework," he said.
   → He said that he ______ his homework.
   A. has finished B. had finished C. finished D. finishes

**答案：1.B 2.B 3.B**
