# 第12单元：推测与可能性（过去）

## 学习目标

完成本单元后，你能够：
1. 掌握must have done, may have done, might have done, can't have done的用法
2. 掌握should have done, ought to have done的用法
3. 理解needn't have done的用法
4. 在过去语境中正确使用推测表达

---

## 一、导入（Lead-in）

### 思考题

请看以下句子，思考它们的含义：

1. He **must have left**.（他一定已经走了。）
2. She **may have forgotten**.（她可能忘了。）
3. He **can't have said** that.（他不可能说了那话。）
4. You **should have told** me.（你本应该告诉我的。）

这些句子表达了什么意思？

**答案**：
- must have left：对过去的肯定推测
- may have forgotten：对过去的不确定推测
- can't have said：对过去的否定推测
- should have told：过去应该做但没做

---

## 二、讲解（Presentation）

### 2.1 结构

**情态动词 + have + 过去分词**

用于对**过去发生的事情**进行推测或评价。

### 2.2 must have done：对过去的肯定推测

must have done表示**对过去事情的肯定推测**，意思是"一定做了"。

**例句**：
1. He **must have left**. His bag is gone.（他一定走了。他的包不见了。）
2. She **must have forgotten** about the meeting.（她一定忘了开会的事。）
3. They **must have arrived** by now.（他们现在一定到了。）
4. It **must have rained** last night. The ground is wet.（昨晚一定下雨了。地是湿的。）

**依据**：通常有证据支持推测。

### 2.3 may/might/could have done：对过去的不确定推测

may/might/could have done表示**对过去事情的不确定推测**，意思是"可能做了"。

**例句**：
1. He **may have left**. I'm not sure.（他可能走了。我不确定。）
2. She **might have forgotten** about the meeting.（她也许忘了开会的事。）
3. They **could have arrived** by now.（他们可能已经到了。）
4. It **may have rained** last night.（昨晚可能下雨了。）

**确定程度**：may > might > could

### 2.4 can't have done：对过去的否定推测

can't have done表示**对过去事情的否定推测**，意思是"不可能做了"。

**例句**：
1. He **can't have left**. His bag is still here.（他不可能走了。他的包还在这儿。）
2. She **can't have forgotten** about the meeting. She wrote it down.（她不可能忘了开会的事。她记下来了。）
3. They **can't have arrived** yet. The flight is delayed.（他们不可能已经到了。航班延误了。）
4. It **can't have rained** last night. The ground is dry.（昨晚不可能下雨了。地是干的。）

### 2.5 should have done：过去应该做但没做

should have done表示**过去应该做但没做**，含有责备或遗憾的意思。

**例句**：
1. You **should have told** me.（你本应该告诉我的。——但你没告诉）
2. She **should have studied** harder.（她本应该更努力学习。——但她没有）
3. I **should have gone** to the party.（我本应该去派对的。——但我没去）
4. He **should have been** more careful.（他本应该更小心的。——但他没有）

**否定形式**：shouldn't have done（不应该做但做了）
1. You **shouldn't have told** him.（你不应该告诉他的。——但你告诉了）
2. I **shouldn't have eaten** so much.（我不应该吃这么多的。——但我吃了）

### 2.6 ought to have done：与should have done相同

ought to have done与should have done意思相同，但更正式。

**例句**：
1. You **ought to have told** me.（你本应该告诉我的。）
2. She **ought to have studied** harder.（她本应该更努力学习。）

### 2.7 needn't have done：本不必做但做了

needn't have done表示**本不必做但做了**。

**例句**：
1. You **needn't have waited** for me.（你本不必等我的。——但你等了）
2. She **needn't have cooked** so much food.（她本不必做这么多菜的。——但她做了）
3. I **needn't have bought** this book.（我本不必买这本书的。——但我买了）

**对比**：
- needn't have done：本不必做但做了
- didn't need to do：不必做（通常也没做）
  - I **didn't need to** go, so I stayed home.（我不必去，所以我待在家。）

---

## 三、辨析（Analysis）

### 3.1 中文母语者常见错误

#### 错误1：混淆must have done和can't have done

- ✗ He **mustn't have left**.（想说"不可能走了"）
- ✓ He **can't have left**.

**原因**：must表示推测时，否定用can't。

#### 错误2：should have done的时态

- ✗ You **should told** me.
- ✓ You **should have told** me.

**原因**：should have + 过去分词，不能省略have。

#### 错误3：混淆needn't have done和didn't need to

- ✗ I **needn't have gone** yesterday, so I stayed home.（想说"不必去，所以没去"）
- ✓ I **didn't need to** go yesterday, so I stayed home.

**原因**：needn't have done表示做了但不必做；didn't need to表示不必做（通常没做）。

#### 错误4：推测过去用现在时

- ✗ He **must leave** yesterday.
- ✓ He **must have left** yesterday.

**原因**：对过去的推测用情态动词 + have + 过去分词。

### 3.2 特殊情况

#### 情况1：could have done表示过去的可能性

could have done可以表示**过去可能发生但没发生**。

**例句**：
1. I **could have helped** you, but I didn't know.（我本可以帮助你的，但我不知道。）
2. She **could have passed** the exam if she had studied.（如果她学习了，她本可以通过考试的。）

#### 情况2：might have done表示过去的可能性

might have done可以表示**过去可能发生**。

**例句**：
1. He **might have gone** home.（他可能回家了。）
2. She **might have forgotten**.（她可能忘了。）

## 四、练习（Practice）

### 练习1：选择合适的情态动词

根据语境，选择must have, may have, might have, could have, can't have填空。

1. The ground is wet. It ______ rained last night. (非常确定)
2. I'm not sure. He ______ left already. (不太确定)
3. His bag is here. He ______ left yet. (非常确定否定)
4. She didn't study. She ______ passed the exam. (非常确定否定)
5. I don't know. She ______ forgotten. (不太确定)

**答案**：
1. must have
2. may have / might have / could have
3. can't have
4. can't have
5. may have / might have / could have

### 练习2：should have done练习

用should have done或shouldn't have done完成句子。

1. You ______ (tell) me about the party. (但你没告诉)
2. I ______ (eat) so much. (但我吃了)
3. She ______ (study) harder. (但她没有)
4. He ______ (be) more careful. (但他没有)
5. They ______ (leave) so early. (但他们离开了)

**答案**：
1. should have told
2. shouldn't have eaten
3. should have studied
4. should have been
5. shouldn't have left

### 练习3：改错

找出并改正以下句子中的错误。

1. He mustn't have left yet.
2. You should told me.
3. She must have forget about the meeting.
4. I needn't have go yesterday, so I stayed home.
5. He can have said that.

**答案**：
1. He **can't have** left yet.
2. You **should have told** me.
3. She must have **forgotten** about the meeting.
4. I **didn't need to** go yesterday, so I stayed home.
5. He **can't have** said that.

### 练习4：翻译练习

将以下中文句子翻译成英文。

1. 他一定已经走了。他的包不见了。
2. 她可能忘了开会的事。
3. 你本应该告诉我的。
4. 昨晚不可能下雨了。地是干的。
5. 你本不必等我的。

**参考答案**：
1. He **must have left**. His bag is gone.

## 六、总结（Summary）

### 核心要点

1. **结构**：情态动词 + have + 过去分词

2. **推测**：
   - must have done：一定做了（肯定推测）
   - may/might/could have done：可能做了（不确定推测）
   - can't have done：不可能做了（否定推测）

3. **评价**：
   - should have done：应该做但没做
   - shouldn't have done：不应该做但做了
   - needn't have done：本不必做但做了

### 对比表格

| 结构 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| must have done | 一定做了 | He must have left. |
| may have done | 可能做了 | He may have left. |
| might have done | 也许做了 | He might have left. |
| could have done | 可能做了 | He could have left. |
| can't have done | 不可能做了 | He can't have left. |
| should have done | 应该做但没做 | You should have told me. |
| shouldn't have done | 不应该做但做了 | You shouldn't have told him. |
| needn't have done | 本不必做但做了 | You needn't have waited. |
