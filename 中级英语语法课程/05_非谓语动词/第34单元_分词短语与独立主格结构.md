# 第34单元 分词短语与独立主格结构

## 学习目标
1. 掌握分词短语作状语的用法
2. 理解独立主格结构的构成和用法
3. 掌握with + 独立主格结构
4. 能够识别和避免悬垂分词

## 一、导入 (Lead-in)

### 思考问题
- "Walking down the street, the trees were beautiful." 这句话对吗？
- 独立主格结构和分词短语有什么区别？

### 对比观察
- Walking down the street, I saw many trees.（走在街上，我看到很多树。）→ 分词短语
- The weather being fine, we went for a walk.（天气很好，我们去散步。）→ 独立主格
- With the weather being fine, we went for a walk.（随着天气变好……）→ with结构

## 二、讲解 (Presentation)

### 2.1 分词短语作状语

**分词短语 = 分词 + 宾语/状语**
- Walking down the street, I met an old friend.
- Being ill, he stayed at home.
- Having finished the work, he went home.

**注意：** 分词短语的逻辑主语必须和句子主语一致
- ✅ Walking down the street, I met an old friend.（I在走路）
- ❌ Walking down the street, the trees were beautiful.（树不会走路）

### 2.2 独立主格结构

**构成：名词/代词 + 分词/形容词/副词/介词短语/不定式**

**分词型独立主格：**
- The weather being fine, we went for a walk.
- The work finished, he went home.
- He came in, book in hand.

**不定式型独立主格：**
- So many people to help, he felt grateful.

**介词短语型独立主格：**
- He sat there, book in hand.
- She came in, with a smile on her face.

### 2.3 with + 独立主格结构

**with + 名词 + 分词/形容词/副词/介词短语**

| 类型 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| with + 名词 + 现在分词 | He sat there with the TV on. |
| with + 名词 + 过去分词 | He sat there with his eyes closed. |
| with + 名词 + 形容词 | He sat there with the window open. |
| with + 名词 + 介词短语 | He sat there with a book in his hand. |

**例句：**
- She came in with a smile on her face.（她面带微笑走进来。）
- He slept with the window open.（他开着窗户睡觉。）
- With so much work to do, I can't go out.（有这么多工作要做，我不能出去。）

### 2.4 悬垂分词（错误用法）

**悬垂分词 = 分词短语的逻辑主语与句子主语不一致**

❌ Walking down the street, the trees were beautiful.
✅ Walking down the street, I found the trees beautiful.

❌ Being ill, his mother took him to the doctor.
✅ Being ill, he was taken to the doctor by his mother.

## 三、辨析 (Analysis)

### 常见错误

1. ❌ Walking down the street, the trees were beautiful.
   ✅ Walking down the street, I found the trees beautiful.
   **分析：** 分词短语的逻辑主语必须是句子主语

2. ❌ The weather fine, we went for a walk.
   ✅ The weather being fine, we went for a walk.
   **分析：** 独立主格结构中，分词不能省略

3. ❌ He sat there with his eyes closing.
   ✅ He sat there with his eyes closed.
   **分析：** 眼睛是被闭上的，用过去分词

### 关键辨析

**分词短语 vs 独立主格**
| 特征 | 分词短语 | 独立主格 |
|------|---------|---------|
| 主语 | 与句子主语一致 | 有自己的主语 |
| 形式 | 分词 + 宾语/状语 | 名词 + 分词/形容词等 |
| 例句 | Walking down... | The weather being... |

## 四、练习 (Practice)

### 练习1：判断正误
判断下列句子是否正确：
1. Being ill, his mother took him to the doctor.
2. The work finished, he went home.
3. Walking down the street, the trees were beautiful.

### 练习2：用独立主格改写
1. Because the weather was fine, we went for a walk.
2. He sat there and his eyes were closed.
3. If time permits, we will finish the work.

### 练习3：用with结构完成句子
1. He slept ______ (the window/open).
2. She came in ______ (a smile/on her face).
3. He sat there ______ (a book/in his hand).

## 五、产出 (Production)

### 任务1：描写场景
用独立主格结构描写一个场景（4-5句）。
例：The sun setting behind the mountains, the sky turned red. The birds flying home, the forest became quiet.

### 任务2：写日记
用with结构写一段日记（3-4句）。
例：I went to school with my bag on my back. I sat in class with my eyes on the teacher.

## 六、总结 (Summary)

### 关键要点
1. 分词短语的逻辑主语必须与句子主语一致
2. 独立主格 = 名词 + 分词/形容词/副词/介词短语
3. with + 独立主格结构更常见
4. 避免悬垂分词

### 自测题
1. ______, we went for a walk.
   A. The weather being fine B. The weather fine
   C. Being fine D. The weather was fine

2. He sat there with his eyes ______.
   A. close B. closing C. closed D. closes

3. ______, the trees were beautiful.
   A. Walking down the street B. I walking down the street
   C. When I walking D. I was walking

**答案：1.A 2.C 3.A（需改为 I walking... 或 Walking..., I found...）**
