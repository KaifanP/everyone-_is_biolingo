# 第31单元 动名词基础

## 学习目标
1. 理解动名词的形式和特征
2. 掌握动名词作主语、宾语、表语的用法
3. 掌握常用接动名词的动词
4. 理解动名词与不定式的区别

## 一、导入 (Lead-in)

### 思考问题
- 动名词和现在分词有什么区别？
- 为什么有些动词后面必须接doing？

### 对比观察
- Swimming is good exercise.（游泳是好运动。）→ 动名词作主语
- He is swimming.（他在游泳。）→ 现在分词构成进行时

## 二、讲解 (Presentation)

### 2.1 动名词的基本形式
- **形式：** 动词 + ing（doing, going, eating）
- **否定形式：** not + doing
- **特征：** 具有名词性质，同时保留动词特征

### 2.2 动名词的句法功能

**1. 作主语**
- Swimming is good exercise.
- Learning English is important.
- It is no use crying over spilt milk.（谚语）

**2. 作宾语**
- I enjoy reading books.
- She finished writing the report.
- He suggested going to the cinema.

**3. 作表语**
- My hobby is collecting stamps.
- Her job is teaching English.

**4. 作介词宾语**
- I am interested in learning Chinese.
- She is good at singing.
- Thank you for helping me.

### 2.3 常接动名词的动词

| 类别 | 动词 |
|------|------|
| 喜欢/享受 | enjoy, love, like, prefer |
| 完成/停止 | finish, stop, quit, give up |
| 建议/考虑 | suggest, consider, mind |
| 继续/保持 | keep, continue, practice |
| 避免/错过 | avoid, miss, delay |
| 其他 | imagine, risk, deny, admit, appreciate |

**例句：**
- I enjoy listening to music.
- She avoided making mistakes.
- He suggested taking a taxi.

### 2.4 介词后的动名词

**常见搭配：**
- be good at doing: She is good at singing.
- be interested in doing: I am interested in learning.
- look forward to doing: I look forward to seeing you.
- be used to doing: He is used to getting up early.
- instead of doing: I stayed home instead of going out.
- thank you for doing: Thank you for helping me.
- without doing: He left without saying goodbye.

## 三、辨析 (Analysis)

### 常见错误

1. ❌ I enjoy to read books.
   ✅ I enjoy reading books.
   **分析：** enjoy后面必须接doing

2. ❌ She suggested to go to the cinema.
   ✅ She suggested going to the cinema.
   **分析：** suggest后面接doing

3. ❌ I look forward to see you.
   ✅ I look forward to seeing you.
   **分析：** look forward to中的to是介词，后面接doing

### 关键辨析

**接doing的动词 vs 接to do的动词**
| 接doing（已发生/习惯） | 接to do（未发生/将来） |
|----------------------|---------------------|
| enjoy, finish, avoid | want, hope, decide |
| suggest, mind, keep | plan, agree, refuse |
| consider, imagine | offer, promise, fail |

## 四、练习 (Practice)

### 练习1：选择doing或to do
1. I enjoy ______ (swim/swimming) in summer.
2. She decided ______ (go/going) abroad.
3. He suggested ______ (take/taking) a taxi.

### 练习2：用动名词完成句子
1. ______ (learn) English is important.
2. I am good at ______ (speak) English.
3. Thank you for ______ (help) me.

### 练习3：改正错误
1. I avoid to make mistakes.
2. She is good at to sing.
3. I look forward to hear from you.

## 五、产出 (Production)

### 任务1：描述你的爱好
用动名词描述你的3个爱好。
例：My hobby is collecting stamps. I enjoy reading books.

### 任务2：给建议
用动名词给朋友3条建议。
例：I suggest taking a break. I recommend studying every day.

## 六、总结 (Summary)

### 关键要点
1. 动名词 = 动词 + ing，具有名词性质
2. 可作主语、宾语、表语、介词宾语
3. 常接动名词的动词：enjoy, finish, avoid, suggest等
4. 介词后面必须接动名词

### 自测题
1. I enjoy ______ tennis.
   A. play B. to play C. playing D. played

2. She is good at ______.
   A. sing B. to sing C. singing D. sang

3. I look forward to ______ you.
   A. see B. to see C. seeing D. seen

**答案：1.C 2.C 3.C**
