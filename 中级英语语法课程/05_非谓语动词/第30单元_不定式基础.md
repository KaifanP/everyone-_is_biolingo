# 第30单元 不定式基础

## 学习目标
1. 理解不定式的两种形式：to do 和 do（省略to）
2. 掌握不定式作主语、宾语、宾补的用法
3. 掌握常用接不定式的动词
4. 理解不定式的时态和语态变化

## 一、导入 (Lead-in)

### 思考问题
- 为什么有些动词后面接to do，有些接doing？
- "I want to go" 和 "I enjoy going" 有什么区别？

### 对比观察
- I want to go.（我想去。）→ 想做某事（未发生）
- I enjoy going.（我喜欢去。）→ 喜欢做某事（已发生/习惯）

## 二、讲解 (Presentation)

### 2.1 不定式的基本形式
- **肯定形式：** to + 动词原形（to do, to go, to eat）
- **否定形式：** not + to + 动词原形（not to do）
- **省略to：** 在某些动词后省略to（let, make, help等）

### 2.2 不定式的句法功能

**1. 作主语**
- To learn English is important.
- It is important to learn English.（it作形式主语）

**2. 作宾语**
- I want to go home.
- She decided to study abroad.

**3. 作宾语补足语**
- I want you to help me.
- The teacher asked us to be quiet.

**4. 作定语（修饰名词）**
- I have a lot of work to do.
- She is the first person to arrive.

**5. 作状语（表目的/结果）**
- I came here to study.（目的）
- He grew up to be a doctor.（结果）

### 2.3 常接不定式的动词

| 类别 | 动词 |
|------|------|
| 意愿/希望 | want, wish, hope, expect, would like |
| 决定/计划 | decide, plan, choose, intend |
| 同意/拒绝 | agree, refuse, promise, offer |
| 努力/尝试 | try, attempt, manage |
| 学习/知道 | learn, know (how) |
| 其他 | seem, appear, afford, pretend, fail |

**例句：**
- I want to travel around the world.
- She decided to quit her job.
- He promised to help me.

### 2.4 省略to的不定式（动词原形）

**使役动词 + 宾语 + do（省略to）**
- let sb do: Let me go.
- make sb do: He made me laugh.
- have sb do: I had him fix my car.

**感官动词 + 宾语 + do**
- see sb do: I saw him leave.
- hear sb do: I heard her sing.
- watch sb do: I watched them play.

**注意：** 被动语态中要还原to
- He was made to work hard.
- She was seen to enter the building.

## 三、辨析 (Analysis)

### 常见错误

1. ❌ I want go home.
   ✅ I want to go home.
   **分析：** want后面必须接to do

2. ❌ He made me to work hard.
   ✅ He made me work hard.
   **分析：** make后省略to

3. ❌ I enjoy to play tennis.
   ✅ I enjoy playing tennis.
   **分析：** enjoy后面接doing，不接to do

### 关键辨析

**to do vs doing 的动词分类**
| 接to do的动词 | 接doing的动词 |
|--------------|--------------|
| want, hope, decide | enjoy, finish, avoid |
| plan, agree, refuse | suggest, mind, practice |
| offer, promise, fail | keep, consider, imagine |

## 四、练习 (Practice)

### 练习1：选择to do或doing
1. I want ______ (go/going) to the cinema.
2. She enjoys ______ (read/reading) novels.
3. He decided ______ (study/studying) abroad.

### 练习2：用不定式完成句子
1. I came here ______ (study) English.
2. She is the first person ______ (arrive).
3. It is important ______ (be) on time.

### 练习3：改正错误
1. He made me to work overtime.
2. I saw him to leave the building.
3. She wants that I help her.

## 五、产出 (Production)

### 任务1：描述你的计划
用不定式描述你未来的5个计划。
例：I plan to learn a new language next year.

### 任务2：给建议
用不定式给朋友3条建议。
例：I advise you to study harder.

## 六、总结 (Summary)

### 关键要点
1. 不定式 = to + 动词原形
2. 可作主语、宾语、宾补、定语、状语
3. 使役动词和感官动词后省略to
4. 被动语态中要还原to

### 自测题
1. She decided ______ abroad.
   A. go B. to go C. going D. went

2. He made me ______ hard.
   A. to work B. work C. working D. worked

3. It is important ______ English well.
   A. learn B. to learn C. learning D. learned

**答案：1.B 2.B 3.B**
