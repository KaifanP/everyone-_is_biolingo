# 第38单元 省略句与替代

## 学习目标
1. 掌握常见的省略句型
2. 理解省略的条件和规则
3. 掌握常见替代词的用法（do/so/not等）
4. 能够在阅读和写作中正确识别和使用省略

## 一、导入 (Lead-in)

### 思考问题
- 为什么英语中经常省略重复的部分？
- 省略句有什么修辞效果？

### 对比观察
- 完整句：He is a student and she is a student.
- 省略句：He is a student and she is too.
- **效果：** 省略使句子更简洁

## 二、讲解 (Presentation)

### 2.1 并列句中的省略

**省略重复的主语、谓语或宾语**
- He is a student and (he) is very smart.（省略主语）
- I like English and (I like) Chinese.（省略主语和谓语）
- He can swim and she can (swim) too.（省略谓语）

**注意：** 省略后句子仍然完整

### 2.2 从句中的省略

**1. 状语从句中的省略**
- When (I was) young, I liked playing football.
- While (he was) reading, he fell asleep.
- If (it is) possible, please come early.
- Although (he was) tired, he continued working.

**条件：** 从句主语与主句主语一致，且从句谓语含be

**2. 定语从句中的省略**
- The book (that/which) I bought is interesting.（省略关系代词）
- The man (who/whom) I met is my teacher.（省略关系代词）

**条件：** 关系代词作宾语时可省略

### 2.3 不定式中的省略

**省略to后面的部分**
- You can go if you want to (go).
- I don't want to go, but I have to (go).
- He didn't come, though I asked him to (come).

### 2.4 替代词的使用

**1. do/does/did 替代前面的动词**
- He works harder than I do.（= work）
- She likes English more than he does.（= likes）

**2. so/not 替代从句**
- "Is it going to rain?" "I think so."（= it is going to rain）
- "Is he coming?" "I think not."（= he is not coming）

**常用搭配：**
- I think so / I think not
- I hope so / I hope not
- I believe so / I believe not
- I'm afraid so / I'm afraid not

**3. one/ones 替代名词**
- I need a pen. Do you have one?（= a pen）
- I like the red ones.（= the red books/apples等）

## 三、辨析 (Analysis)

### 常见错误

1. ❌ When young, my father liked football.
   ✅ When young, I liked football.
   **分析：** 省略时，从句主语必须与主句主语一致

2. ❌ He works harder than I work.
   ✅ He works harder than I do.
   **分析：** 用do替代重复的动词

3. ❌ "Is he coming?" "I think not so."
   ✅ "Is he coming?" "I think not."
   **分析：** not本身就是替代词

### 关键辨析

**省略 vs 替代**
| 类型 | 特征 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 省略 | 直接删除重复部分 | He is a student and she is too. |
| 替代 | 用替代词代替 | He works harder than I do. |

## 六、总结 (Summary)

### 关键要点
1. 并列句中省略重复的主语、谓语或宾语
2. 状语从句中省略（主语一致 + 谓语含be）
3. 定语从句中省略关系代词（作宾语时）
4. do/does/did替代动词，so/not替代从句

### 自测题
1. When ______, I liked playing.
   A. young B. I young C. was young D. I was young

2. He works harder than I ______.
   A. work B. do C. am D. did

3. "Is he coming?" "I ______."
   A. think so B. think it C. think that D. think

**答案：1.A 2.B 3.A**
