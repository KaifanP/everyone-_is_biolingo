# 第37单元 强调句

## 学习目标
1. 掌握It is/was...that/who...强调句型
2. 掌握do/does/did强调谓语动词的用法
3. 理解强调句与其他句型的区别
4. 能够在写作中正确使用强调句

## 一、导入 (Lead-in)

### 思考问题
- 如何强调句子中的某个成分？
- "It was John who broke the window" 和 "John broke the window" 有什么区别？

### 对比观察
- 普通句：John broke the window yesterday.
- 强调主语：It was John who broke the window yesterday.
- 强调宾语：It was the window that John broke yesterday.
- 强调时间：It was yesterday that John broke the window.

## 二、讲解 (Presentation)

### 2.1 It is/was...that/who... 强调句型

**结构：It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 其他**

**强调人：** 用who或that
- It was John who/that broke the window.（是约翰打破了窗户。）

**强调物/时间/地点：** 用that
- It was the window that John broke.（约翰打破的是窗户。）
- It was yesterday that John broke the window.（约翰是昨天打破窗户的。）
- It was at school that John broke the window.（约翰是在学校打破窗户的。）

**注意：** 强调句去掉It is/was和that/who后，句子仍然完整

### 2.2 do/does/did 强调谓语动词

**结构：主语 + do/does/did + 动词原形**

- I do like English.（我确实喜欢英语。）
- He does work hard.（他确实工作很努力。）
- She did come yesterday.（她昨天确实来了。）

**注意：** 只用于一般现在时和一般过去时

### 2.3 强调句的疑问形式

**一般疑问句：**
- Was it John who broke the window?
- Is it English that you like?

**特殊疑问句：**
- Who was it that broke the window?
- What is it that you want?
- When was it that you arrived?
- Where was it that you met him?

### 2.4 强调句的否定形式

- It was not John who broke the window.（打破窗户的不是约翰。）
- It was not yesterday that he arrived.（他不是昨天到的。）

## 三、辨析 (Analysis)

### 常见错误

1. ❌ It was John which broke the window.
   ✅ It was John who/that broke the window.
   **分析：** 强调人用who或that，不用which

2. ❌ It is English what I like.
   ✅ It is English that I like.
   **分析：** 强调物用that，不用what

3. ❌ It was yesterday when he arrived.
   ✅ It was yesterday that he arrived.
   **分析：** 强调时间用that，不用when

### 关键辨析

**强调句 vs 主语从句**
| 句型 | 结构 | 判断方法 |
|------|------|---------|
| 强调句 | It is/was...that... | 去掉后句子完整 |
| 主语从句 | It is...that... | 去掉后句子不完整 |

**例句：**
- 强调句：It was John that broke the window. → John broke the window. ✅
- 主语从句：It is clear that he is wrong. → Clear he is wrong. ❌

## 四、练习 (Practice)

### 练习1：用强调句改写
强调划线部分：
1. **John** broke the window yesterday.
2. John broke **the window** yesterday.
3. John broke the window **yesterday**.

### 练习2：判断是否为强调句
判断下列句子是否为强调句：
1. It is clear that he is wrong.
2. It was John that broke the window.
3. It was at school that I met him.

### 练习3：用do/does/did强调
1. I like English. → I ______ like English.
2. He works hard. → He ______ work hard.
3. She came yesterday. → She ______ come yesterday.

## 五、产出 (Production)

### 任务1：强调练习
用强调句改写以下句子，强调不同的成分：
> Tom met Mary at the park yesterday.

### 任务2：写作练习
用强调句写一段短文（4-5句），描述一个重要的事件。

## 六、总结 (Summary)

### 关键要点
1. It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 其他
2. 强调人用who/that，强调物/时间/地点用that
3. do/does/did 强调谓语动词
4. 去掉强调结构后句子仍然完整

### 自测题
1. It was John ______ broke the window.
   A. which B. who C. what D. when

2. It was yesterday ______ he arrived.
   A. when B. where C. that D. which

3. I ______ like English.
   A. do B. does C. did D. am

**答案：1.B 2.C 3.A**
