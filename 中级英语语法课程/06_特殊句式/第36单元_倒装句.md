# 第36单元 倒装句

## 学习目标
1. 理解倒装句的类型：全部倒装和部分倒装
2. 掌握常见的倒装句型
3. 能够在写作中正确使用倒装句
4. 掌握考试中常见的倒装句考点

## 一、导入 (Lead-in)

### 思考问题
- 为什么英语中有时要把助动词放在主语前面？
- 倒装句有什么修辞效果？

### 对比观察
- 正常语序：I have never seen such a beautiful sunset.
- 倒装语序：Never have I seen such a beautiful sunset.
- **效果：** 倒装句更强调"never"，语气更强

## 二、讲解 (Presentation)

### 2.1 全部倒装

**全部倒装 = 整个谓语动词放在主语前面**

**1. 地点/方位副词开头**
- Here comes the bus.（公交车来了。）
- There goes the bell.（铃响了。）
- Away flew the bird.（鸟飞走了。）

**注意：** 主语是代词时不倒装
- ✅ Here it comes.（它来了。）
- ❌ Here comes it.

**2. 表语前置**
- Present at the meeting was the headmaster.（出席会议的是校长。）
- Gone are the days when we had to walk to school.（我们不得不步行上学的日子一去不复返了。）

### 2.2 部分倒装

**部分倒装 = 助动词/情态动词放在主语前面**

**1. 否定词/半否定词开头**
- Never have I seen such a thing.（我从未见过这样的事。）
- Seldom does he go out.（他很少出门。）
- Hardly had I arrived when it started to rain.（我刚到就开始下雨了。）
- No sooner had I arrived than it started to rain.（我刚到就开始下雨了。）
- Not only did he come, but he also helped.（他不仅来了，还帮了忙。）
- Not until midnight did he finish the work.（直到半夜他才完成工作。）

**2. only + 状语开头**
- Only then did I realize my mistake.（直到那时我才意识到我的错误。）
- Only in this way can you succeed.（只有这样你才能成功。）
- Only after he left did I notice the letter.（他走了之后我才注意到那封信。）

**3. so/such...that 结构**
- So beautiful was the sunset that everyone stopped to watch.（日落太美了，每个人都停下来观看。）
- Such was his anger that he couldn't speak.（他愤怒得说不出话来。）

**4. so/neither/nor 表示"也..."**
- He is a student, and so am I.（他是学生，我也是。）
- He can't swim, and neither can I.（他不会游泳，我也不会。）

**5. 虚拟条件句省略if**
- Had I known, I would have come.（如果我知道，我就来了。）
- Were I you, I would accept.（如果我是你，我就接受。）
- Should it rain, we would cancel.（如果下雨，我们就取消。）

## 三、辨析 (Analysis)

### 常见错误

1. ❌ Never I have seen such a thing.
   ✅ Never have I seen such a thing.
   **分析：** 否定词开头需要部分倒装

2. ❌ Here comes it.
   ✅ Here it comes.
   **分析：** 主语是代词时不倒装

3. ❌ Only then I realized my mistake.
   ✅ Only then did I realize my mistake.
   **分析：** only + 状语开头需要部分倒装

### 关键辨析

**全部倒装 vs 部分倒装**
| 类型 | 特征 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 全部倒装 | 整个谓语在主语前 | Here comes the bus. |
| 部分倒装 | 助动词在主语前 | Never have I seen... |

## 四、练习 (Practice)

### 练习1：将下列句子改为倒装句
1. I have never seen such a beautiful sunset.
2. He not only came, but he also helped.
3. Only then did I realize my mistake.
4. He is a student, and I am also a student.

### 练习2：选择正确形式
1. Never ______ such a thing.
   A. I have seen B. have I seen C. I saw D. did I saw

2. Here ______.
   A. comes the bus B. the bus comes C. does the bus come D. is the bus coming

3. Only in this way ______ succeed.
   A. you can B. can you C. you will D. will you

### 练习3：改正错误
1. Hardly I had arrived when it started to rain.
2. So beautiful the sunset was that everyone stopped.
3. Not only he came, but he also helped.

## 五、产出 (Production)

### 任务1：写作练习
用倒装句写一段短文（4-5句），描述一个令人难忘的场景。

### 任务2：改写练习
将以下段落改写为使用倒装句：
> I had never seen such a beautiful place. The scenery was so beautiful that I couldn't speak. Only when I left did I realize how much I loved it.

## 六、总结 (Summary)

### 关键要点
1. 全部倒装：地点副词/表语前置
2. 部分倒装：否定词/only/so/such开头
3. so/neither/nor 表示"也..."
4. 虚拟条件句省略if时倒装

### 自测题
1. Not only ______, but he also helped.
   A. he came B. did he come C. he did come D. came he

2. ______ I known, I would have come.
   A. Have B. Had C. If D. Should

3. So beautiful ______ that everyone stopped.
   A. was the sunset B. the sunset was C. is the sunset D. the sunset is

**答案：1.B 2.B 3.A**
