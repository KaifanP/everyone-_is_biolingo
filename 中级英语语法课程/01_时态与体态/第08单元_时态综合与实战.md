## 一、导入（Lead-in）

### 综合测试

请判断以下段落中每个动词的时态，并说明为什么使用该时态。

> By the time I **arrived** at the station, the train **had** already **left**. I **had been waiting** for over an hour, and I **was** exhausted. I **decided** to take a taxi instead. The driver **told** me that he **had been working** since early morning. He **said** he **would** **take** me to the airport. By the time we **got** there, I **had been traveling** for three hours. I **knew** I **would have missed** my flight if I **had waited** for the next train.

**分析**：
1. **arrived**：一般过去时——过去的事实
2. **had left**：过去完成时——在我到达之前（过去的过去）
3. **had been waiting**：过去完成进行时——在我到达之前一直在等（持续）
4. **was**：一般过去时——过去的事实
5. **decided**：一般过去时——过去的动作
6. **told**：一般过去时——过去的动作
7. **had been working**：过去完成进行时——在告诉我之前一直在工作（持续）
8. **said**：一般过去时——过去的动作
9. **would take**：过去将来时——从过去看将来
10. **got**：一般过去时——过去的动作
11. **had been traveling**：过去完成进行时——在到达机场之前一直在旅行（持续）
12. **knew**：一般过去时——过去的事实
13. **would have missed**：过去将来完成时——从过去看将来的假设
14. **had waited**：过去完成时——虚拟条件（与过去事实相反）

## 二、讲解（Presentation）

### 2.1 时态选择的核心原则

选择时态时，问自己三个问题：

**问题1：动作发生在什么时候？**
- 过去 → 用过去时态
- 现在 → 用现在时态
- 将来 → 用将来时态

**问题2：动作处于什么状态？**
- 一般事实 → 一般时态
- 正在进行 → 进行时态
- 已完成 → 完成时态
- 持续进行 → 完成进行时态

**问题3：有没有时间标志词？**
- 有 → 根据标志词选择
- 没有 → 根据语境判断

### 2.2 时态一致性（Sequence of Tenses）

在复杂句中，主句和从句的时态需要保持一致。

#### 规则1：主句过去时，从句也用过去时态

**例句**：
- He **said** that he **was** tired.
- She **told** me that she **had finished** her work.
- I **thought** that he **would come**.

**注意**：如果从句表达的是客观事实或习惯，可以保持现在时。
- He **said** that the earth **is** round.（客观事实）
- She **told** me that she **drinks** coffee every morning.（习惯）

#### 规则2：主句现在时，从句可用任何时态

**例句**：
- I **think** that he **is** tired.
- I **think** that he **was** tired yesterday.
- I **think** that he **will come** tomorrow.

### 2.3 叙事文中的时态使用

在叙事文中，时态的使用有一定的规律。

#### 一般过去时：叙述主要事件

**例句**：
- I **woke** up at 7 am.
- I **had** breakfast.
- I **went** to work.
- I **met** my boss.
- We **discussed** the project.

#### 过去进行时：描述背景

**例句**：
- It **was raining** when I left home.
- She **was reading** a book while waiting.
- They **were having** dinner when the phone rang.

#### 过去完成时：叙述更早的事件

**例句**：
- I **had finished** my work before the meeting.
- She **had already left** when I arrived.
- He **had never been** to Paris before that trip.

#### 过去完成进行时：强调持续

**例句**：
- I **had been waiting** for an hour when he came.
- She **had been working** all day, so she was tired.

### 2.4 学术写作中的时态使用

在学术写作中，时态的使用有特定的规范。

#### 一般现在时：普遍事实、理论

**例句**：
- The earth **revolves** around the sun.
- Water **boils** at 100°C.
- This theory **states** that...

#### 一般过去时：研究方法、实验结果

**例句**：
- We **conducted** an experiment.
- The results **showed** that...
- The participants **completed** a survey.

#### 现在完成时：已有研究、现状

**例句**：
- Previous studies **have shown** that...
- Researchers **have found** that...
- This issue **has been** widely studied.

#### 将来时：未来研究方向

**例句**：
- Future research **will focus** on...
- We **will investigate** this further.

## 三、辨析（Analysis）

### 3.1 高频易错时态对比

#### 对比1：现在完成时 vs 一般过去时

| 现在完成时 | 一般过去时 |
|------------|------------|
| have/has done | did |
| 与现在有关 | 与现在无关 |
| I have lost my key. | I lost my key yesterday. |

#### 对比2：现在完成时 vs 现在完成进行时

| 现在完成时 | 现在完成进行时 |
|------------|----------------|
| have/has done | have/has been doing |
| 强调结果 | 强调过程 |
| I have read the book. | I have been reading the book. |

#### 对比3：过去完成时 vs 一般过去时

| 过去完成时 | 一般过去时 |
|------------|------------|
| had done | did |
| 过去的过去 | 过去 |
| When I arrived, she had left. | I arrived at 8 pm. |

#### 对比4：will vs be going to

| will | be going to |
|------|-------------|
| 临时决定 | 计划好的 |
| I will help you. | I am going to visit Paris. |

### 3.2 中文母语者常见错误总结

#### 错误1：忘记时态变化

- ✗ I **go** to Beijing last year.
- ✓ I **went** to Beijing last year.

#### 错误2：过度使用进行时

- ✗ I **am knowing** the answer.
- ✓ I **know** the answer.

#### 错误3：完成时与过去时混淆

- ✗ I **have gone** to Beijing last year.
- ✓ I **went** to Beijing last year.

#### 错误4：将来时态选择错误

- ✗ I **will visit** Paris next month. (如果是计划好的)
- ✓ I **am going to visit** Paris next month.

#### 错误5：时间从句中用将来时

- ✗ When I **will arrive**, I will call you.
- ✓ When I **arrive**, I will call you.

## 四、练习（Practice）

### 练习1：综合时态填空

用括号内动词的正确时态填空。

1. By the time I ______ (get) to the airport, the plane ______ (already/leave).
2. She ______ (work) at this company for five years before she ______ (resign).
3. I ______ (read) when the phone ______ (ring).
4. He ______ (tell) me that he ______ (see) the movie before.
5. By next year, I ______ (live) here for ten years.
6. She ______ (always/complain) about the weather.
7. I ______ (meet) him at the party, but I ______ (meet) him before that.
8. When I ______ (be) young, I ______ (play) football every day.
9. By the time you ______ (arrive), I ______ (finish) cooking.
10. He ______ (work) all day, so he ______ (be) tired.

**答案**：
1. got, had already left
2. had worked / had been working, resigned
3. was reading, rang
4. told, had seen
5. will have been living / will have lived
6. is always complaining
7. met, had met
8. was, played
9. arrive, will have finished
10. had been working, was

### 练习2：段落改错

找出并改正以下段落中的时态错误。

> Last week, I go to the cinema with my friend. We have decided to see a new movie. When we arrive, the movie already start. We miss the beginning. My friend tell me that he see the movie before. He say it is very good. I enjoy the movie. By the time we leave, we have been there for two hours.

**改正**：
> Last week, I **went** to the cinema with my friend. We **had decided** to see a new movie. When we **arrived**, the movie **had** already **started**. We **missed** the beginning. My friend **told** me that he **had seen** the movie before. He **said** it **was** very good. I **enjoyed** the movie. By the time we **left**, we **had been** there for two hours.

### 练习3：时态综合练习

根据语境，用正确的时态完成句子。

1. I ______ (study) English for ten years, and I ______ (still/learn) new things.
2. She ______ (work) at the company since 2015, and she ______ (never/think) about leaving.
3. By the time I ______ (graduate), I ______ (complete) 40 courses.
4. He ______ (always/forget) his keys. He ______ (lose) them three times this week.
5. When I ______ (arrive) at the party, everyone ______ (already/eat). They ______ (wait) for me for an hour.

**答案**：
1. have been studying, am still learning
2. has worked / has been working, has never thought
3. graduate, will have completed
4. is always forgetting, has lost
5. arrived, had already eaten, had been waiting

### 练习4：翻译练习

将以下中文句子翻译成英文，注意时态。

1. 我一早上都在学习英语。
2. 她告诉我她以前去过那里。
3. 到明年，我将在这里工作满十年。
4. 我到达时，他已经离开了。
5. 她总是在抱怨天气。
6. 我认为明天会下雨。
7. 他一直在工作，所以他很累。
8. 到月底，我将读完五本书。

**参考答案**：
1. I **have been studying** English all morning.
2. She told me she **had been** there before.
3. By next year, I **will have been working** here for ten years.
4. When I arrived, he **had** already **left**.
5. She **is always complaining** about the weather.
6. I think it **will rain** tomorrow.
7. He **had been working**, so he was tired. / He **has been working**, so he is tired.
8. By the end of the month, I **will have read** five books.

### 任务2：时态转换练习

**原文（过去时）**：
> I woke up at 7 am. I had breakfast. I went to work. I met my boss. We discussed the project. I finished my work at 5 pm. I went home.

> I wake up at 7 am. I have breakfast. I go to work. I meet my boss. We discuss the project. I finish my work at 5 pm. I go home.

> I will wake up at 7 am. I will have breakfast. I will go to work. I will meet my boss. We will discuss the project. I will finish my work at 5 pm. I will go home.

## 六、总结（Summary）

### 核心要点

1. **时态选择三问**：
   - 动作发生在什么时候？
   - 动作处于什么状态？
   - 有没有时间标志词？

2. **时态一致性**：
   - 主句过去时，从句也用过去时态
   - 客观事实例外

3. **叙事文中的时态**：
   - 一般过去时：主要事件
   - 过去进行时：背景描述
   - 过去完成时：更早的事件
   - 过去完成进行时：持续的动作

4. **学术写作中的时态**：
   - 一般现在时：普遍事实
   - 一般过去时：研究方法
   - 现在完成时：已有研究

### 时态总表

| 时态 | 结构 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| 一般现在时 | do/does | 习惯、事实 | I work every day. |
| 现在进行时 | am/is/are doing | 正在进行 | I am working now. |
| 现在完成时 | have/has done | 与现在有关 | I have finished. |
| 现在完成进行时 | have/has been doing | 持续到现在 | I have been working. |
| 一般过去时 | did | 过去事实 | I worked yesterday. |
| 过去进行时 | was/were doing | 过去进行 | I was working at 8 pm. |
| 过去完成时 | had done | 过去的过去 | I had finished before. |
| 过去完成进行时 | had been doing | 过去持续 | I had been working. |
| 一般将来时 | will do | 将来事实 | I will work tomorrow. |
| 将来进行时 | will be doing | 将来进行 | I will be working at 8 pm. |
| 将来完成时 | will have done | 将来完成 | I will have finished. |
| 将来完成进行时 | will have been doing | 将来持续 | I will have been working. |
