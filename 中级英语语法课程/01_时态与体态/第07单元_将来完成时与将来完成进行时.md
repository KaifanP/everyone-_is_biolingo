# 第7单元：将来完成时与将来完成进行时

## 学习目标

完成本单元后，你能够：
1. 掌握将来完成时的结构和用法
2. 掌握将来完成进行时的结构和用法
3. 区分两种时态的差异
4. 在复杂语境中正确使用这些时态

---

## 一、导入（Lead-in）

### 思考题

请看以下两组句子，思考它们的区别：

**第一组**：
1. By next year, I **will have finished** my degree.（到明年，我将完成我的学位。）
2. By next year, I **will have been studying** for five years.（到明年，我将学了五年了。）

**第二组**：
1. By the time you arrive, I **will have cooked** dinner.（你到的时候，我将做好晚饭。）
2. By the time you arrive, I **will have been cooking** for two hours.（你到的时候，我将做了两个小时饭了。）

这两组句子有什么不同？

**答案**：
- 第1句（将来完成时）：强调**完成的结果**
- 第2句（将来完成进行时）：强调**持续的过程**

---

## 二、讲解（Presentation）

### 2.1 将来完成时（Future Perfect）

**结构**：will have + 过去分词

**肯定句**：主语 + will have + 过去分词
- I **will have finished** by tomorrow.
- She **will have left** before you arrive.

**否定句**：主语 + will not have + 过去分词
- I **will not have finished** by then.
- She **will not have left** yet.

**疑问句**：Will + 主语 + have + 过去分词?
- **Will** you **have finished** by tomorrow?
- **Will** she **have left** before I arrive?

#### 用法1：在将来某个时间点之前完成

表示**在将来某个时间点之前**已经完成的动作。

**例句**：
1. By next year, I **will have finished** my degree.
   - 到明年，我将完成我的学位

2. By the time you arrive, I **will have cooked** dinner.
   - 你到的时候，我将做好晚饭

3. By 2025, she **will have worked** here for ten years.
   - 到2025年，她将在这里工作十年

4. By the end of this month, I **will have read** ten books.
   - 到这个月底，我将读完十本书

**时间标志词**：by the time, by next year/month, by + 将来时间, by the end of

#### 用法2：对过去的推测（不确定）

表示**对过去事情的推测**，但不确定。

**例句**：
1. She **will have arrived** by now.（她现在应该到了。）
2. They **will have finished** lunch.（他们应该吃完午饭了。）
3. He **will have heard** the news.（他应该听到消息了。）

**注意**：这种用法与"对现在的推测"相似，但强调动作已经完成。

### 2.2 将来完成进行时（Future Perfect Continuous）

**结构**：will have been + doing

**肯定句**：主语 + will have been + doing
- I **will have been working** here for ten years by December.
- She **will have been studying** English for five years by next year.

**否定句**：主语 + will not have been + doing
- I **will not have been waiting** that long.

**疑问句**：Will + 主语 + have been + doing?
- **Will** you **have been working** here for ten years by then?

#### 用法：在将来某个时间点之前一直在进行

表示**在将来某个时间点之前**一直在进行的动作，强调**持续性**。

**例句**：
1. By December, I **will have been working** here for ten years.
   - 到12月，我将在这里工作满十年

2. By the time she retires, she **will have been teaching** for thirty years.
   - 她退休时，将教了三十年书

3. By next Monday, I **will have been waiting** for two weeks.
   - 到下周一，我将等了两周

4. By the end of the year, he **will have been living** in London for five years.
   - 到年底，他将在伦敦住了五年

**时间标志词**：by the time, by + 将来时间, for + 时间段

### 2.3 将来完成时 vs 将来完成进行时

这是本单元的重点。

#### 对比1：完成 vs 持续

| 将来完成时 | 将来完成进行时 |
|------------|----------------|
| will have done | will have been doing |
| 强调**完成的结果** | 强调**持续的过程** |
| 动作已完成 | 动作可能还在进行 |

**例句对比**：
- By next year, I **will have finished** my degree.（强调完成）
- By next year, I **will have been studying** for five years.（强调持续）

- By the time you arrive, I **will have cooked** dinner.（强调结果——晚饭做好了）
- By the time you arrive, I **will have been cooking** for two hours.（强调过程——做了两小时）

#### 对比2：次数/量 vs 持续时间

| 将来完成时 | 将来完成进行时 |
|------------|----------------|
| 强调**次数/数量** | 强调**持续时间** |

**例句对比**：
- By the end of the year, I **will have read** ten books.（十本书——数量）
- By the end of the year, I **will have been reading** for six months.（六个月——持续时间）

#### 对比3：状态动词

**状态动词**（know, believe, want等）**不能用进行时**，只能用完成时。

**例句**：
- ✓ By next year, I **will have known** him for ten years.
- ✗ By next year, I **will have been knowing** him for ten years.

### 2.4 实际应用

#### 应用1：学业/工作计划

**例句**：
- By the time I graduate, I **will have completed** 40 courses.
- By the end of this project, we **will have worked** on it for six months.
- By next year, she **will have been working** at the company for five years.

#### 应用2：人生目标

**例句**：
- By the time I'm 30, I **will have traveled** to 20 countries.
- By the time I retire, I **will have saved** enough money.
- By the end of this year, I **will have been learning** English for ten years.

#### 应用3：日常安排

**例句**：
- By the time you get here, I **will have left**.
- By the end of the day, I **will have finished** all my tasks.
- By tomorrow, she **will have been waiting** for a week.

## 三、辨析（Analysis）

### 3.1 中文母语者常见错误

#### 错误1：混淆完成时和完成进行时

- ✗ By next year, I **will have been finishing** my degree.（想说"完成学位"）
- ✓ By next year, I **will have finished** my degree.（强调完成）
- ✓ By next year, I **will have been studying** for five years.（强调持续）

**原因**：完成强调结果，完成进行强调持续。

#### 错误2：状态动词用完成进行时

- ✗ By next year, I **will have been knowing** him for ten years.
- ✓ By next year, I **will have known** him for ten years.

**原因**：know是状态动词，不能用进行时。

#### 错误3：忘记have

- ✗ By next year, I **will finished** my degree.
- ✓ By next year, I **will have finished** my degree.

**原因**：将来完成时的结构是will have + 过去分词，不能省略have。

#### 错误4：在时间从句中用将来完成时

- ✗ When I **will have arrived**, I will call you.
- ✓ When I **arrive**, I will call you.
- ✓ By the time I **arrive**, I will have finished.

**原因**：在when引导的时间从句中，用一般现在时代替将来时。但by the time引导的从句可以用将来完成时。

### 3.2 特殊情况

#### 情况1：by the time vs when

**by the time**：强调"到……时候为止"，通常用完成时
- **By the time** you arrive, I **will have finished**.

**when**：强调"当……时候"，通常用一般时
- **When** you arrive, I **will call** you.

#### 情况2：will have + 过去分词 vs will have been + doing

**例句对比**：
- By 5 pm, I **will have worked** for 8 hours.（强调总时长）
- By 5 pm, I **will have been working** for 8 hours.（强调持续过程）

两句意思相近，但完成进行时更强调**持续性**。

#### 情况3：对过去的推测

将来完成时可以表示**对过去事情的推测**。

**例句**：
- She **will have arrived** by now.（她现在应该到了。）
- They **will have finished** lunch.（他们应该吃完午饭了。）

**注意**：这种用法与"对现在的推测"相似。

## 四、练习（Practice）

### 练习1：将来完成时 vs 将来完成进行时

选择正确的时态填空。

1. By next year, I ______ (finish) my degree.
2. By next year, I ______ (study) for five years.
3. By the time you arrive, I ______ (cook) dinner.
4. By the time you arrive, I ______ (cook) for two hours.
5. By December, she ______ (work) here for ten years.
6. By the end of the month, I ______ (read) ten books.

**答案**：
1. will have finished（强调完成）
2. will have been studying（强调持续）
3. will have cooked（强调结果——饭做好了）
4. will have been cooking（强调过程——做了两小时）
5. will have been working / will have worked（两者皆可）
6. will have read（强调数量）

### 练习2：改错

找出并改正以下句子中的错误。

1. By next year, I will finished my degree.
2. By December, I will have been knowing him for ten years.
3. By the time you arrive, I will have been cooking dinner.
4. When I will have arrived, I will call you.
5. By the end of the year, I will have been read ten books.
6. By next month, she will have been work here for five years.

**答案**：
1. By next year, I **will have finished** my degree.
2. By December, I **will have known** him for ten years.
3. By the time you arrive, I **will have cooked** dinner.（如果强调结果）
4. When I **arrive**, I will call you.
5. By the end of the year, I **will have read** ten books.
6. By next month, she **will have been working** here for five years.

### 练习3：句子改写

用by the time/by改写句子，使用将来完成时或将来完成进行时。

**示例**：
- I will finish my work. Then you can go.
- **By the time I finish my work, you will have been waiting for a long time.**

1. She will leave. Then I will arrive.
2. He will study for five years. Then he will graduate.
3. They will live in London for ten years. Then they will move.
4. I will read three books. Then the month will end.

**参考答案**：
1. **By the time** I arrive, she **will have left**.
2. **By the time** he graduates, he **will have studied** for five years.
3. **By the time** they move, they **will have been living** in London for ten years.
4. **By the end of** the month, I **will have read** three books.

## 六、总结（Summary）

### 核心要点

1. **将来完成时**：will have + 过去分词
   - 在将来某个时间点之前完成的动作
   - 强调完成的结果

2. **将来完成进行时**：will have been + doing
   - 在将来某个时间点之前一直在进行的动作
   - 强调持续的过程

3. **两者对比**：
   - 完成时：强调结果/数量
   - 完成进行时：强调过程/持续时间

4. **状态动词不能用完成进行时**

5. **时间标志词**：by the time, by next year, by + 将来时间, for + 时间段

### 对比表格

| 将来完成时 | 将来完成进行时 |
|------------|----------------|
| will have done | will have been doing |
| 强调完成结果 | 强调持续过程 |
| I will have finished. | I will have been studying. |
| 状态动词可用 | 状态动词不可用 |
