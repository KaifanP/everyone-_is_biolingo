# 第2单元：现在完成时 vs 一般过去时

## 学习目标

完成本单元后，你能够：
1. 清晰区分现在完成时和一般过去时的核心差异
2. 根据语境正确选择时态
3. 掌握两种时态的时间标志词
4. 避免中文母语者常见错误

---

## 一、导入（Lead-in）

### 思考题

请看以下两组句子，思考它们的区别：

**第一组**：
1. I **lost** my key.（我丢了钥匙。）
2. I **have lost** my key.（我丢了钥匙。）

**第二组**：
1. She **went** to Paris.（她去过巴黎。）
2. She **has been** to Paris.（她去过巴黎。）

表面上看，中文翻译一样，但英文含义有什么不同？

**答案**：
- 第一组第1句：只是陈述过去发生的事（也许已经找到了）
- 第一组第2句：强调**现在的影响**（现在还没找到，没钥匙用）
- 第二组第1句：她过去去了巴黎（可能还在那里，或已经回来了）
- 第二组第2句：她有去巴黎的**经历**（现在回来了）

---

## 二、讲解（Presentation）

### 2.1 核心区别

| | 一般过去时 | 现在完成时 |
|------|------------|------------|
| **结构** | did | have/has done |
| **时间** | 过去 | 过去 → 现在 |
| **重点** | 过去的事实 | 与现在的关联 |
| **是否与现在有关** | ❌ 不一定 | ✅ 一定有关 |
| **时间是否结束** | ✅ 结束 | ❌ 可能继续 |

### 2.2 一般过去时：过去的事实

一般过去时**只描述过去发生的事**，不强调与现在的关系。

**例句**：
1. I **visited** Beijing in 2010.
   - 只是说2010年去了北京，现在是否在北京不重要

2. She **lived** in London for ten years.
   - 她在伦敦住过十年（现在不住了）

3. He **worked** at Google from 2015 to 2020.
   - 他在谷歌工作过（现在不工作了）

**关键**：有明确的过去时间（in 2010, from 2015 to 2020）

### 2.3 现在完成时：与现在的关联

现在完成时强调**过去发生的事对现在有影响**，或**从过去持续到现在**。

**用法1：结果对现在有影响**

1. I **have lost** my key.
   - 过去丢的，**现在**没钥匙用

2. She **has broken** her leg.
   - 过去摔的，**现在**腿还断着

3. He **has eaten** lunch.
   - 他吃过午饭了，**现在**不饿

**用法2：经历（life experience）**

1. I **have been** to Paris.
   - 我去过巴黎（人生经历，现在回来了）

2. She **has seen** that movie three times.
   - 她看过那部电影三次（人生经历）

3. **Have** you ever **eaten** sushi?
   - 你吃过寿司吗？（人生经历）

**用法3：从过去持续到现在**

1. I **have lived** here since 2010.
   - 2010年开始住，**现在还住着**

2. She **has worked** at this company for five years.
   - 五年前开始工作，**现在还在工作**

3. We **have known** each other since childhood.
   - 小时候认识，**现在还认识**

### 2.4 时间标志词

#### 只用于一般过去时的标志词

| 标志词 | 例句 |
|--------|------|
| yesterday | I saw him **yesterday**. |
| last night/week/month/year | She called **last night**. |
| ago | He left **two hours ago**. |
| in + 过去年份 | She was born **in 1990**. |
| when I was young | I played a lot **when I was young**. |
| just now | I saw him **just now**. |

#### 只用于现在完成时的标志词

| 标志词 | 例句 |
|--------|------|
| already | I have **already** finished. |
| yet (否定/疑问) | Have you finished **yet**? / I haven't finished **yet**. |
| just | She has **just** left. |
| ever | **Have** you **ever** been to Japan? |
| never | I have **never** seen snow. |
| so far | **So far**, we have learned 100 words. |
| up to now | **Up to now**, everything has been fine. |
| recently | I have **recently** started exercising. |
| since + 时间点 | I have lived here **since 2010**. |
| for + 时间段 | She has worked here **for five years**. |

#### 两者皆可用的标志词

| 标志词 | 说明 |
|--------|------|
| this morning | 如果说话时还在早上，用现在完成时；如果已经是下午/晚上，用过去时 |
| today | 同上 |
| this week/month/year | 如果还没结束，用现在完成时 |

**例句对比**：
- I **have seen** two movies **this week**.（这周还没结束）
- I **saw** two movies **this week**.（这周已经结束，或强调过去）

### 2.5 since vs for

这两个词都表示"持续时间"，但用法不同：

| | since | for |
|------|-------|-----|
| **含义** | 从……开始 | 持续……时间 |
| **后面接** | 时间点 | 时间段 |
| **例句** | since 2010, since Monday, since I was a child | for five years, for two hours, for a long time |

**例句**：
- I have lived here **since 2010**.（从2010年开始）
- I have lived here **for ten years**.（持续了十年）

**常见错误**：
- ✗ I have lived here **since** ten years.
- ✓ I have lived here **for** ten years.

---

## 三、辨析（Analysis）

### 3.1 中文母语者常见错误

#### 错误1：过去时间用现在完成时

- ✗ I **have gone** to Beijing **last year**.
- ✓ I **went** to Beijing **last year**.

**原因**：last year是明确的过去时间，必须用过去时。

#### 错误2：since/for混淆

- ✗ I have lived here **since** five years.
- ✓ I have lived here **for** five years.

**原因**：five years是时间段，用for；since后面必须是时间点。

#### 错误3：have been to vs have gone to

- She **has been** to Paris.（她去过巴黎，现在回来了）
- She **has gone** to Paris.（她去巴黎了，现在不在这儿）

**区别**：
- have been to：经历（去过，回来了）
- have gone to：去了（还没回来）

#### 错误4：状态动词用完成进行时

- ✗ I **have been knowing** him for ten years.
- ✓ I **have known** him for ten years.

**原因**：know是状态动词，不能用进行时。

### 3.2 特殊情况

#### 情况1：just, already, yet

这些词常与现在完成时连用，但美式英语中也可用过去时。

- I **have just** finished.（英式）
- I **just** finished.（美式）

两种都正确，但考试中建议用现在完成时。

#### 情况2：this morning（时间段未结束）

- It's 10 am. I **have had** two cups of coffee **this morning**.（早上还没结束）
- It's 3 pm. I **had** two cups of coffee **this morning**.（早上已结束）

#### 情况3：ago与过去时

ago只能与过去时连用，不能与现在完成时连用。

- ✗ I have seen him **two days ago**.
- ✓ I saw him **two days ago**.

### 3.3 对比练习

请判断以下句子应该用什么时态，并说明原因。

1. I ______ (visit) the Great Wall when I was in Beijing.
2. I ______ (visit) the Great Wall three times.
3. She ______ (live) in Paris from 2010 to 2015.
4. She ______ (live) in Paris since 2010.
5. He ______ (break) his leg last week.
6. He ______ (break) his leg, so he can't play football.

**答案**：
1. **visited** —— when I was in Beijing是过去时间
2. **have visited** —— three times是经历，用现在完成时
3. **lived** —— from...to...是过去时间段，用过去时
4. **has lived** —— since 2010从过去到现在，用现在完成时
5. **broke** —— last week是过去时间
6. **has broken** —— 强调现在影响（不能踢球）

## 四、练习（Practice）

### 练习1：时态选择

选择正确的时态填空。

1. I ______ (already/finish) my homework. Can I go out?
2. She ______ (go) to the supermarket ten minutes ago.
3. ______ you ever ______ (try) Japanese food?
4. He ______ (not/call) me since last Monday.
5. We ______ (meet) at the party last night.
6. I ______ (read) three books this month.
7. She ______ (work) at this company for five years before she resigned.
8. ______ you ______ (see) my phone? I can't find it.

**答案**：
1. have already finished
2. went
3. Have, ever tried
4. hasn't called
5. met
6. have read
7. worked / had worked
8. Have, seen

### 练习2：since vs for

用since或for填空。

1. I have lived here ______ 2015.
2. She has been studying English ______ three years.
3. He has worked at Google ______ last summer.
4. We have known each other ______ a long time.
5. They have been married ______ 2010.
6. I haven't eaten ______ this morning.

**答案**：
1. since
2. for
3. since
4. for
5. since
6. since

### 练习3：have been to vs have gone to

用have been to或have gone to填空。

1. Where is Tom? He ______ the library.
2. I ______ Paris twice. It's a beautiful city.
3. She ______ the bank. She'll be back in ten minutes.
4. ______ you ever ______ China?
5. He ______ Japan three times on business.

**答案**：
1. has gone to
2. have been to
3. has gone to
4. Have, been to
5. has been to

### 练习4：改错

找出并改正以下句子中的时态错误。

1. I have seen him yesterday.
2. She has gone to Paris last summer.
3. He has been knowing her since childhood.
4. I have lived here since five years.
5. They have already left when I arrived.
6. Have you finished your homework yet? Yes, I did it an hour ago.

**答案**：
1. I **saw** him yesterday.
2. She **went** to Paris last summer.
3. He **has known** her since childhood.
4. I have lived here **for** five years.
5. They **had** already left when I arrived.
6. Have you finished your homework yet? Yes, I **finished** it an hour ago.（ago用过去时）

## 六、总结（Summary）

### 核心要点

1. **一般过去时**：只描述过去的事实，不强调与现在的关联
2. **现在完成时**：强调与现在的关联（结果、经历、持续）

3. **时间标志词**：
   - 过去时：yesterday, ago, last, in + 过去年份
   - 现在完成时：already, yet, just, ever, never, since, for, recently

4. **since vs for**：
   - since + 时间点
   - for + 时间段

5. **have been to vs have gone to**：
   - been to：去过（回来了）
   - gone to：去了（没回来）

### 对比表格

| | 一般过去时 | 现在完成时 |
|------|------------|------------|
| 结构 | did | have/has done |
| 时间 | 过去（已结束） | 过去 → 现在 |
| 重点 | 过去事实 | 现在关联 |
| 标志词 | yesterday, ago, last | already, yet, just, since, for |
| 例句 | I saw him yesterday. | I have just seen him. |
