# 第4单元：过去完成时

## 学习目标

完成本单元后，你能够：
1. 理解"过去的过去"这个概念
2. 掌握过去完成时的结构和用法
3. 在叙事中正确使用过去完成时
4. 区分过去完成时和一般过去时

---

## 一、导入（Lead-in）

### 思考题

请看以下故事，思考为什么用了过去完成时：

> When I **arrived** at the station, the train **had** already **left**. I **had** **missed** it because I **had woken** up late. I **hadn't checked** the timetable, so I **didn't know** the train left earlier than usual.

为什么有些动词用过去时（arrived, didn't know），有些用过去完成时（had left, had missed, had woken, hadn't checked）？

**答案**：
- **arrived**：我到达车站（过去某个时间点）
- **had left**：火车在我到达**之前**就开走了（过去的过去）
- **had missed**：我在到达**之前**就错过了（过去的过去）
- **had woken**：我在到达**之前**就起晚了（过去的过去）

**关键**：过去完成时表示**在过去的某个时间点之前**发生的动作。

---

## 二、讲解（Presentation）

### 2.1 结构

**肯定句**：主语 + had + 过去分词（done）

- I **had finished** the work before she arrived.
- She **had left** when I called.
- They **had already eaten** by the time we got there.

**否定句**：主语 + had not (hadn't) + 过去分词

- I **hadn't seen** him before that day.
- She **hadn't finished** when the boss came.

**疑问句**：Had + 主语 + 过去分词?

- **Had** you **met** him before?
- **Had** she **left** when you arrived?

### 2.2 核心概念：过去的过去

过去完成时表示**在过去的某个时间点之前**已经完成的动作。

**时间轴**：

```
过去完成时          一般过去时          现在
    ↓                   ↓               ↓
[动作A发生]  →  [动作B发生]  →  [说话时]
had done            did              now
```

**例句分析**：

1. When I **arrived** (B), she **had left** (A).
   - 时间顺序：先离开（A），后到达（B）
   - 两个都是过去，但A在B之前

2. By the time I **got** home (B), my wife **had cooked** dinner (A).
   - 时间顺序：先做饭（A），后到家（B）

3. I **didn't go** to the movie because I **had seen** it before.
   - 时间顺序：先看过（A），后决定不去（B）

### 2.3 用法

#### 用法1：在另一个过去动作之前完成

这是最常见的用法，表示**在过去某个动作之前**已经完成的动作。

**例句**：
1. When I **arrived**, she **had** already **left**.
   - 我到达时，她已经离开了

2. He **told** me that he **had seen** the movie before.
   - 他告诉我他以前看过那部电影

3. She **didn't want** to go because she **had been** there before.
   - 她不想去，因为她以前去过那里

4. After I **had finished** my work, I **went** home.
   - 我完成工作后，就回家了

**关键词**：when, before, after, by the time, already, just

#### 用法2：在过去某时间之前持续的状态

表示**在过去某个时间点之前**已经持续了一段时间的状态。

**例句**：
1. By 2010, I **had lived** in Beijing for ten years.
   - 到2010年时，我在北京已经住了十年

2. She **had worked** at the company for five years before she resigned.
   - 她辞职前，在那家公司工作了五年

3. When he retired, he **had been** a teacher for thirty years.
   - 他退休时，已经当了三十年老师

#### 用法3：表示过去的经历

在叙述过去事件时，表示**在那之前**的经历。

**例句**：
1. I **had never seen** snow before I went to Harbin.
   - 我去哈尔滨之前，从没见过雪

2. She **hadn't eaten** sushi until she visited Japan.
   - 她去日本之前，从没吃过寿司

3. He **had been** to Paris twice before 2020.
   - 2020年之前，他去过两次巴黎

### 2.4 时间标志词

| 标志词 | 例句 |
|--------|------|
| before | I **had met** him **before**. |
| after | **After** I **had eaten**, I went out. |
| when | **When** I arrived, she **had left**. |
| by the time | **By the time** I got there, he **had gone**. |
| already | She **had** **already** left. |
| just | I **had** **just** finished when he called. |
| never | I **had** **never** seen such a thing. |
| until | I **hadn't** understood **until** she explained. |

### 2.5 过去完成时 vs 一般过去时

| | 一般过去时 | 过去完成时 |
|------|------------|------------|
| **结构** | did | had done |
| **时间** | 过去 | 过去的过去 |
| **含义** | 过去发生的事 | 在过去某时间之前发生的事 |

**例句对比**：
- I **ate** breakfast at 8 am.（我8点吃了早饭。——过去）
- I **had eaten** breakfast before I left home.（我离家前吃了早饭。——过去的过去）

- She **went** to Paris in 2015.（她2015年去了巴黎。——过去）
- She **had been** to Paris before 2015.（她2015年之前去过巴黎。——过去的过去）

## 三、辨析（Analysis）

### 3.1 中文母语者常见错误

#### 错误1：忘记用过去完成时

- ✗ When I arrived, she **left**.（她在我到达时离开了——时间顺序不清）
- ✓ When I arrived, she **had left**.（她在我到达之前就离开了）

**原因**：中文用"已经""之前"等词表示时间顺序，英文需要用时态变化。

#### 错误2：过度使用过去完成时

- ✗ I **had breakfast** at 8 am yesterday.（只是说昨天8点吃了早饭）
- ✓ I **had** breakfast at 8 am yesterday.（这里had是"吃"，不是助动词）

**注意**：had breakfast = 吃早饭，不是过去完成时。

#### 错误3：混淆before/after的用法

**正确**：
- **Before** I **left**, I **had locked** the door.
- I **had locked** the door **before** I **left**.
- **After** I **had locked** the door, I **left**.
- I **left** **after** I **had locked** the door.

**注意**：before/after已经表示时间顺序，所以也可以用一般过去时：
- **Before** I **left**, I **locked** the door.
- **After** I **locked** the door, I **left**.

两种都正确，但用过去完成时更强调时间顺序。

#### 错误4：在when从句中错误使用

**错误**：When I **had arrived**, she left.
**正确**：When I **arrived**, she **had left**.

**原因**：when引导的从句通常是**后发生的动作**，主句是**先发生的动作**。

### 3.2 特殊情况

#### 情况1：叙事中的时间顺序

如果时间顺序很清楚，可以用一般过去时代替过去完成时。

**例句**：
- I **got up**, **brushed** my teeth, **had** breakfast, and **left**.
- I **had got up**, **had brushed** my teeth, **had had** breakfast, and **had left**.

第一句更简洁，因为时间顺序已经很清楚（按顺序发生）。

#### 情况2：间接引语中的过去完成时

转述过去说过的话时，用过去完成时。

**直接引语**：He said, "I **have seen** the movie."
**间接引语**：He said that he **had seen** the movie.

**直接引语**：She said, "I **finished** my homework."
**间接引语**：She said that she **had finished** her homework.

#### 情况3：wish/if only + 过去完成时

表示对过去的遗憾。

**例句**：
- I wish I **had studied** harder.（我希望我以前学习更努力。——事实：没有）
- If only I **had known**!（要是我当时知道就好了！——事实：不知道）

## 四、练习（Practice）

### 练习1：过去完成时填空

用括号内动词的正确时态填空。

1. When I arrived at the cinema, the movie ______ (already/start).
2. She ______ (not/see) snow before she went to Harbin.
3. By the time he graduated, he ______ (study) English for ten years.
4. I ______ (finish) my homework before I went out.
5. He told me that he ______ (meet) her before.
6. After she ______ (eat) dinner, she watched TV.
7. They ______ (just/leave) when I called.
8. I ______ (never/be) to Japan until last year.

**答案**：
1. had already started
2. hadn't seen
3. had studied / had been studying
4. had finished
5. had met
6. had eaten
7. had just left
8. had never been

### 练习2：选择正确的时态

选择一般过去时或过去完成时填空。

1. I ______ (go) to bed after I ______ (finish) my work.
2. When she ______ (arrive), the party ______ (already/start).
3. He ______ (not/know) the answer because he ______ (not/study).
4. By the time I ______ (get) to the airport, the plane ______ (leave).
5. She ______ (tell) me that she ______ (see) the movie before.
6. I ______ (meet) him at the party, but I ______ (meet) him before that.
7. After he ______ (graduate), he ______ (find) a job.
8. When I ______ (be) young, I ______ (play) football every day.

**答案**：
1. went, had finished
2. arrived, had already started
3. didn't know, hadn't studied
4. got, had left
5. told, had seen
6. met, had met（先见过，后在派对上又见）
7. graduated, found / had graduated, found
8. was, played

### 练习3：改错

找出并改正以下句子中的错误。

1. When I arrived, she already left.
2. I had saw that movie before.
3. By the time I got there, he has left.
4. After I had ate dinner, I went for a walk.
5. She told me she has finished the work.
6. I had been to Paris before I visit it last year.

**答案**：
1. When I arrived, she **had** already **left**.
2. I had **seen** that movie before.
3. By the time I got there, he **had** left.
4. After I had **eaten** dinner, I went for a walk.
5. She told me she **had** finished the work.
6. I had been to Paris before I **visited** it last year.

### 练习4：句子合并

用before/after/when/by the time将两个句子合并为一个句子。

**示例**：
- I finished my work. I went home.
- **After I had finished my work, I went home.**

1. She ate dinner. She watched TV.
2. He graduated. He found a job.
3. I arrived. The train left.
4. We got to the cinema. The movie started.

**参考答案**：
1. **After she had eaten dinner**, she watched TV. / She watched TV **after she had eaten dinner**.
2. **After he had graduated**, he found a job. / He found a job **after he had graduated**.
3. **When I arrived**, the train **had** already **left**.
4. **By the time we got to the cinema**, the movie **had** already **started**.

### 练习5：翻译练习

将以下中文句子翻译成英文。

1. 我到达时，他已经离开了。
2. 她告诉我她以前去过那里。
3. 到2020年时，他已经学了十年英语。
4. 我做完作业后才出去玩。
5. 要是我早知道就好了。

**参考答案**：
1. When I arrived, he **had** already **left**.
2. She told me she **had been** there before.
3. By 2020, he **had studied** English for ten years.
4. I didn't go out to play until I **had finished** my homework.
5. If only I **had known** earlier!

## 六、总结（Summary）

### 核心要点

1. **过去完成时 = 过去的过去**
   - 结构：had + 过去分词
   - 表示在过去某个时间点之前发生的动作

2. **用法**：
   - 在另一个过去动作之前完成
   - 在过去某时间之前持续的状态
   - 过去的经历

3. **时间标志词**：before, after, when, by the time, already, just, never

4. **中文母语者常见错误**：
   - 忘记用过去完成时
   - 过度使用
   - before/after从句中的时态

### 时间轴

```
过去完成时 (had done)  →  一般过去时 (did)  →  现在
      ↓                       ↓                ↓
   动作A                   动作B             说话时
 (先发生)                (后发生)
```
