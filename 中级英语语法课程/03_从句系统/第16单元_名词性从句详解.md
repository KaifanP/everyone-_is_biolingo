# 第16单元：名词性从句详解

## 学习目标

完成本单元后，你能够：
1. 掌握whether/if引导的名词性从句
2. 掌握wh-词引导的名词性从句
3. 理解名词性从句中的虚拟语气
4. 在复杂语境中正确使用名词性从句

---

## 一、导入（Lead-in）

### 思考题

请看以下句子，思考引导词的作用：

1. I don't know **whether he will come**.
2. I don't know **what he said**.
3. **Whether he will come** is unknown.
4. **What he said** surprised me.

这些引导词有什么作用？

**答案**：
- whether：是否
- what：什么（在从句中作成分）

---

## 二、讲解（Presentation）

### 2.1 whether/if引导的名词性从句

#### 基本用法

whether和if都表示"是否"，引导名词性从句。

**例句**：
1. I don't know **whether/if he will come**.（我不知道他是否会来。）
2. **Whether he will come** is unknown.（他是否会来还不知道。）
3. The question is **whether he will come**.（问题是他是否会来。）

#### whether vs if的区别

| 情况 | whether | if |
|------|---------|-----|
| 宾语从句 | ✓ | ✓ |
| 主语从句 | ✓ | ✗ |
| 表语从句 | ✓ | ✗ |
| 同位语从句 | ✓ | ✗ |
| 介词后 | ✓ | ✗ |
| or not紧随其后 | ✓ | ✗ |

**例句对比**：
- ✓ **Whether he will come** is unknown.（主语从句）
- ✗ **If he will come** is unknown.

- ✓ I don't know **whether or not he will come**.（or not紧随）
- ✗ I don't know **if or not he will come**.

- ✓ It depends on **whether he will come**.（介词后）
- ✗ It depends on **if he will come**.

### 2.2 wh-词引导的名词性从句

wh-词（what, who, where, when, why, how等）可以引导名词性从句。

#### what引导的名词性从句

what在从句中作主语、宾语、表语。

**例句**：
1. **What he said** surprised me.（他说的话让我惊讶。——作主语）
2. I don't know **what he said**.（我不知道他说了什么。——作宾语）
3. The question is **what he said**.（问题是他说了什么。——作表语）

#### who/whom引导的名词性从句

who/whom在从句中作主语/宾语。

**例句**：
1. **Who will come** is unknown.（谁会来还不知道。——作主语）
2. I don't know **who will come**.（我不知道谁会来。——作主语）
3. I don't know **whom he invited**.（我不知道他邀请了谁。——作宾语）

#### where/when/why/how引导的名词性从句

这些词在从句中作状语。

**例句**：
1. I don't know **where he lives**.（我不知道他住在哪里。）
2. I don't know **when he will come**.（我不知道他什么时候会来。）
3. I don't know **why he left**.（我不知道他为什么离开。）
4. I don't know **how he did it**.（我不知道他怎么做的。）

### 2.3 名词性从句中的语序

**重要**：名词性从句用**陈述语序**，不用疑问语序。

**例句**：
- ✗ I don't know **where does he live**.
- ✓ I don't know **where he lives**.

- ✗ I don't know **what is he doing**.
- ✓ I don't know **what he is doing**.

- ✗ **What did he say** surprised me.
- ✓ **What he said** surprised me.

### 2.4 名词性从句中的虚拟语气

某些动词/形容词后的名词性从句用虚拟语气。

#### suggest/recommend/demand/insist等动词

**结构**：动词 + that + 主语 + (should) + 动词原形

**例句**：
1. I suggest **that he (should) go**.（我建议他去。）
2. She recommended **that we (should) study** harder.（她建议我们更努力学习。）
3. He demanded **that she (should) leave**.（他要求她离开。）
4. They insisted **that he (should) come**.（他们坚持要他来。）

**常见动词**：suggest, recommend, demand, insist, require, propose, request, order

#### important/necessary/essential等形容词

**结构**：It is + 形容词 + that + 主语 + (should) + 动词原形

**例句**：
1. It is important **that you (should) study** hard.（你努力学习很重要。）
2. It is necessary **that he (should) come**.（他来是必要的。）
3. It is essential **that she (should) be** on time.（她准时是必要的。）

**常见形容词**：important, necessary, essential, vital, crucial, imperative

## 三、辨析（Analysis）

### 3.1 中文母语者常见错误

#### 错误1：名词性从句用疑问语序

- ✗ I don't know **where does he live**.
- ✓ I don't know **where he lives**.

#### 错误2：if用于主语从句

- ✗ **If he will come** is unknown.
- ✓ **Whether he will come** is unknown.

#### 错误3：虚拟语气中用should

- ✗ I suggest that he **should goes**.
- ✓ I suggest that he **(should) go**.

#### 错误4：混淆what和that

- ✗ I know **what** he is right.（想说"我知道他是对的"）
- ✓ I know **that** he is right.

**区分**：that在从句中不作成分；what在从句中作成分。

### 3.2 特殊情况

#### 情况1：what引导的名词性从句

what = the thing that，在从句中作成分。

**例句**：
- **What he said** surprised me. = **The thing that he said** surprised me.
- I don't know **what he said**. = I don't know **the thing that he said**.

#### 情况2：whoever/whatever/whichever

这些词表示"无论谁/什么/哪个"。

**例句**：
1. **Whoever comes** is welcome.（无论谁来都欢迎。）
2. You can take **whatever you want**.（你想要什么就拿什么。）
3. **Whichever you choose** is fine.（你选哪个都可以。）

## 四、练习（Practice）

### 练习1：whether/if选择

选择whether或if填空。

1. I don't know ______ he will come.
2. ______ he will come is unknown.
3. It depends on ______ he will come.
4. I don't know ______ or not he will come.
5. The question is ______ he will come.

**答案**：
1. whether/if
2. Whether
3. whether
4. whether
5. whether

### 练习2：语序改正

改正以下句子中的语序错误。

1. I don't know where does he live.
2. What did he say surprised me.
3. I don't know what is he doing.
4. Who will come is unknown.
5. I don't know how did he do it.

**答案**：
1. I don't know where **he lives**.
2. **What he said** surprised me.
3. I don't know what **he is doing**.
4. Who will come is unknown.（正确）
5. I don't know how **he did it**.

### 练习3：虚拟语气练习

用虚拟语气完成句子。

1. I suggest that he ______ (go).
2. It is important that she ______ (be) on time.
3. He demanded that she ______ (leave).
4. It is necessary that we ______ (study) hard.
5. They insisted that he ______ (come).

**答案**：
1. (should) go
2. (should) be
3. (should) leave
4. (should) study
5. (should) come

### 练习4：翻译练习

将以下中文句子翻译成英文。

1. 我不知道他是否会来。
2. 他说的话让我惊讶。
3. 我建议他努力学习。
4. 谁会来还不知道。
5. 你努力学习很重要。

**参考答案**：
1. I don't know **whether/if he will come**.
2. **What he said** surprised me.
3. I suggest that he **(should) study** hard.
4. **Who will come** is unknown.
5. It is important that you **(should) study** hard.

## 五、总结（Summary）

### 核心要点

1. **whether/if引导的名词性从句**：
   - 表示"是否"
   - 主语从句只能用whether

2. **wh-词引导的名词性从句**：
   - what, who, where, when, why, how
   - 在从句中作成分

3. **语序**：名词性从句用陈述语序

4. **虚拟语气**：suggest/important等后的从句用动词原形

### 对比表格

| 引导词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|--------|------|------|
| that | （无意义） | I know that he is right. |
| whether | 是否 | I don't know whether he will come. |
| if | 是否 | I don't know if he will come. |
| what | 什么 | I know what he said. |
| who | 谁 | I know who will come. |
| where | 哪里 | I know where he lives. |
| when | 什么时候 | I know when he will come. |
| why | 为什么 | I know why he left. |
| how | 怎样 | I know how he did it. |
