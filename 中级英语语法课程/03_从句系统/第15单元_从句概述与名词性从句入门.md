## 一、导入（Lead-in）

### 思考题

请看以下句子，思考划线部分的作用：

1. **What he said** surprised me.
2. I don't know **whether he will come**.
3. The fact is **that he is wrong**.
4. The news **that he won** made us happy.

这些划线部分在句子中充当什么成分？

**答案**：
1. 主语从句——作主语
2. 宾语从句——作宾语
3. 表语从句——作表语
4. 同位语从句——解释说明名词

---

## 二、讲解（Presentation）

### 2.1 从句的概念

**从句**是句子中充当某个成分的句子。它有主语和谓语，但不能独立存在，必须依附于主句。

**例句**：
- 主句：I know something.
- 从句：**that he is right**（他正确）
- 合并：I know **that he is right**.

### 2.2 从句的类型

| 从句类型 | 功能 | 例句 |
|----------|------|------|
| 名词性从句 | 作名词（主语、宾语、表语、同位语） | I know **that he is right**. |
| 定语从句 | 作形容词（修饰名词） | The man **who came** is my friend. |
| 状语从句 | 作副词（修饰动词、句子） | I'll go **if he goes**. |

### 2.3 名词性从句概述

名词性从句在句子中充当名词的角色。

| 类型 | 功能 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 主语从句 | 作主语 | **What he said** surprised me. |
| 宾语从句 | 作宾语 | I know **that he is right**. |
| 表语从句 | 作表语 | The fact is **that he is wrong**. |
| 同位语从句 | 解释名词 | The news **that he won** made us happy. |

### 2.4 that引导的名词性从句

that是最常用的引导词，引导陈述性的名词性从句。

#### 主语从句

**that从句作主语**，通常用it作形式主语。

**例句**：
1. **That he is right** is obvious.（他正确是显而易见的。）
2. **It** is obvious **that he is right**.（用it作形式主语）
3. **That she passed the exam** surprised everyone.（她通过考试让每个人都很惊讶。）
4. **It** surprised everyone **that she passed the exam**.

**常用句型**：
- It is + 形容词 + that...
  - It is important **that** you study hard.
- It is + 名词 + that...
  - It is a fact **that** the earth is round.
- It + 动词 + that...
  - It seems **that** he is tired.

#### 宾语从句

**that从句作宾语**，that可以省略。

**例句**：
1. I know **(that)** he is right.（我知道他是对的。）
2. She said **(that)** she would come.（她说她会来。）
3. I think **(that)** he is tired.（我认为他累了。）
4. He believes **(that)** she is honest.（他相信她是诚实的。）

**注意**：在正式写作中，that通常不省略。

#### 表语从句

**that从句作表语**。

**例句**：
1. The fact is **that** he is wrong.（事实是他错了。）
2. The problem is **that** we don't have enough time.（问题是我们没有足够的时间。）
3. My opinion is **that** we should go.（我的意见是我们应该去。）

#### 同位语从句

**that从句作同位语**，解释说明前面的名词。

**例句**：
1. The news **that he won** made us happy.（他赢了的消息让我们很高兴。）
2. The fact **that he is wrong** is obvious.（他错了的事实是显而易见的。）
3. The idea **that we should go** is good.（我们应该去的想法是好的。）

**常见名词**：news, fact, idea, belief, hope, thought, suggestion, report

**同位语从句 vs 定语从句**：
- 同位语从句：解释名词内容（that不作成分）
- 定语从句：修饰名词（that作成分）

**例句对比**：
- The news **that he won** is true.（同位语——解释news的内容）
- The news **that he told me** is true.（定语——that作told的宾语）

## 三、辨析（Analysis）

### 3.1 中文母语者常见错误

#### 错误1：that不可省略的情况

**主语从句中that不可省略**：
- ✗ **He is right** is obvious.
- ✓ **That he is right** is obvious.

**表语从句中that通常不省略**：
- ✗ The fact is **he is wrong**.
- ✓ The fact is **that he is wrong**.

#### 错误2：同位语从句和定语从句混淆

- The news **that he won** is true.（同位语——news的内容）
- The news **that he told me** is true.（定语——that作told的宾语）

**区分方法**：看that在从句中是否作成分。

#### 错误3：it作形式主语的错误

- ✗ **That he is right** is obvious. (口语中常用，但正式写作建议用it)
- ✓ **It** is obvious **that he is right**.

#### 错误4：宾语从句的语序

- ✗ I know **that is he right**.
- ✓ I know **that he is right**.

**原因**：宾语从句用陈述语序，不用疑问语序。

### 3.2 特殊情况

#### 情况1：that从句作介词宾语

介词后面通常不直接接that从句，需要加the fact。

**例句**：
- ✗ I am worried **that** he is sick.
- ✓ I am worried about **the fact that** he is sick.
- ✓ I am worried **that** he is sick.（口语中可接受）

#### 情况2：虚拟语气中的that从句

某些动词后的that从句用虚拟语气（动词原形）。

**例句**：
- I suggest **that he go**.（我建议他去。）
- It is important **that she be** on time.（她准时很重要。）

**常见动词**：suggest, recommend, demand, insist, require, propose

## 四、练习（Practice）

### 练习1：名词性从句类型判断

判断以下句子中从句的类型（主语从句、宾语从句、表语从句、同位语从句）。

1. I know **that he is right**.
2. **That he is right** is obvious.
3. The fact is **that he is wrong**.
4. The news **that he won** made us happy.
5. **It** is important **that you study hard**.
6. She said **that she would come**.
7. The idea **that we should go** is good.
8. **It** seems **that he is tired**.

**答案**：
1. 宾语从句
2. 主语从句
3. 表语从句
4. 同位语从句
5. 主语从句（it作形式主语）
6. 宾语从句
7. 同位语从句
8. 主语从句（it作形式主语）

### 练习2：句子合并

用that将两个句子合并。

**示例**：
- He is right. This is obvious.
- **That he is right is obvious.** / **It is obvious that he is right.**

1. She passed the exam. This surprised everyone.
2. He will come. I know this.
3. The earth is round. This is a fact.
4. He won the game. The news made us happy.

**答案**：
1. **That she passed the exam** surprised everyone. / **It** surprised everyone **that she passed the exam**.
2. I know **that he will come**.
3. **It** is a fact **that the earth is round**. / The fact is **that the earth is round**.
4. The news **that he won the game** made us happy.

### 练习3：改错

找出并改正以下句子中的错误。

1. He is right is obvious.
2. The fact is he is wrong.
3. I know that is he right.
4. The news that he told me is about his winning.
5. I suggest that he goes.

**答案**：
1. **That** he is right is obvious.
2. The fact is **that** he is wrong.
3. I know that **he is** right.
4. The news **that he won** is about his winning. (同位语)
5. I suggest that he **go**. (虚拟语气)

### 练习4：翻译练习

将以下中文句子翻译成英文。

1. 他是对的，这是显而易见的。
2. 我知道她会来。
3. 事实是他错了。
4. 他赢了的消息让我们很高兴。
5. 你努力学习很重要。

**参考答案**：
1. **It** is obvious **that he is right**.
2. I know **that she will come**.
3. The fact is **that he is wrong**.
4. The news **that he won** made us happy.
5. **It** is important **that you study hard**.

## 五、产出（Production）

### 任务1：句子改写

用it作形式主语改写以下句子。

**示例**：
- That he is right is obvious. → **It** is obvious **that he is right**.

1. That she passed the exam surprised everyone.
2. That he is tired is clear.
3. That we should go is my opinion.
4. That the earth is round is a fact.

**答案**：
1. **It** surprised everyone **that she passed the exam**.
2. **It** is clear **that he is tired**.
3. **It** is my opinion **that we should go**.
4. **It** is a fact **that the earth is round**.

## 六、总结（Summary）

### 核心要点

1. **从句类型**：
   - 名词性从句：作名词
   - 定语从句：作形容词
   - 状语从句：作副词

2. **名词性从句类型**：
   - 主语从句：作主语
   - 宾语从句：作宾语
   - 表语从句：作表语
   - 同位语从句：解释名词

3. **that引导的名词性从句**：
   - 主语从句：That he is right is obvious.
   - 宾语从句：I know that he is right.
   - 表语从句：The fact is that he is wrong.
   - 同位语从句：The news that he won is true.

4. **it作形式主语**：It is obvious that he is right.

### 对比表格

| 从句类型 | 功能 | 例句 |
|----------|------|------|
| 主语从句 | 作主语 | That he is right is obvious. |
| 宾语从句 | 作宾语 | I know that he is right. |
| 表语从句 | 作表语 | The fact is that he is wrong. |
| 同位语从句 | 解释名词 | The news that he won is true. |
