# 第21单元：状语从句——原因、目的、结果

## 学习目标

完成本单元后，你能够：
1. 掌握原因状语从句的引导词（because/since/as/for）
2. 掌握目的状语从句的用法（so that/in order that）
3. 掌握结果状语从句的用法（so...that/such...that）
4. 区分不同引导词的语气和用法

---

## 一、导入（Lead-in）

### 思考题

请看以下句子，思考划线部分的作用：

1. I stayed at home **because I was sick**.
2. **Since you are tired**, let's take a break.
3. He spoke quietly **so that no one could hear**.
4. It was **so cold that** the river froze.

这些划线部分分别表示什么？

**答案**：
1. 原因——因为我生病了
2. 原因——既然你累了
3. 目的——以便没有人能听到
4. 结果——冷到河流结冰

---

## 二、讲解（Presentation）

### 2.1 原因状语从句

原因状语从句表示动作发生的原因，由because/since/as/for等引导。

#### because：因为

**用法**：语气最强，表示直接原因，可以回答why的问题。

**例句**：
1. I stayed at home **because I was sick**.（我待在家里因为我生病了。）
2. **Because it was raining**, we cancelled the picnic.（因为下雨，我们取消了野餐。）
3. She was late **because the traffic was heavy**.（她迟到了因为交通拥堵。）

**注意**：because不能与so连用。
- ✗ **Because** it was raining, **so** we stayed home.
- ✓ **Because** it was raining, we stayed home.
- ✓ It was raining, **so** we stayed home.

#### since：既然 / 因为

**用法**：语气较弱，表示已知原因或显而易见的原因。

**例句**：
1. **Since you are tired**, let's take a break.（既然你累了，我们休息一下。）
2. **Since it's late**, we should go home.（既然很晚了，我们应该回家。）
3. I'll help you **since you helped me**.（既然你帮过我，我也会帮你。）

#### as：由于 / 因为

**用法**：语气较弱，表示已知原因。

**例句**：
1. **As it was getting dark**, we decided to go home.（由于天快黑了，我们决定回家。）
2. **As you are busy**, I won't disturb you.（既然你很忙，我就不打扰你了。）
3. She didn't come **as she was ill**.（她没来因为她生病了。）

#### for：因为（补充说明）

**用法**：语气最弱，用于补充说明原因，不能放在句首。

**例句**：
1. He must be at home, **for the light is on**.（他一定在家，因为灯亮着。）
2. She must be sick, **for she looks pale**.（她一定生病了，因为她看起来很苍白。）

**注意**：for是并列连词，不能放在句首。

#### because/since/as/for对比

| 引导词 | 语气 | 位置 | 用法 |
|--------|------|------|------|
| because | 最强 | 句首/句中 | 直接原因，回答why |
| since | 较弱 | 句首/句中 | 已知原因 |
| as | 较弱 | 句首/句中 | 已知原因 |
| for | 最弱 | 只在句中 | 补充说明 |

### 2.2 目的状语从句

目的状语从句表示动作的目的，由so that/in order that等引导。

#### so that：以便

**用法**：表示"以便……"，从句中常用can/could/will/would/may/might等情态动词。

**例句**：
1. He spoke quietly **so that no one could hear**.（他小声说话以便没有人能听到。）
2. I'll give you my number **so that you can call me**.（我把号码给你以便你能给我打电话。）
3. She studied hard **so that she could pass the exam**.（她努力学习以便能通过考试。）

#### in order that：为了

**用法**：比so that更正式，意思相同。

**例句**：
1. He worked hard **in order that he could support his family**.（他努力工作以便能养家。）
2. **In order that everyone could hear**, he spoke slowly.（为了让大家都能听到，他说得很慢。）

#### 目的 vs 结果

| | 目的状语从句 | 结果状语从句 |
|------|------------|------------|
| 引导词 | so that | so...that |
| 含义 | 以便（目的） | 如此……以至于（结果） |
| 情态动词 | 常用can/could/will/would | 不用情态动词 |
| 可否省略 | 可以省略 | 不能省略 |

**例句对比**：
- 目的：He spoke quietly **so that** no one **could** hear.（他小声说话以便没人听到。）
- 结果：He spoke **so** quietly **that** no one heard.（他说得太小声了，以至于没人听到。）

### 2.3 结果状语从句

结果状语从句表示动作的结果，由so...that/such...that等引导。

#### so...that：如此……以至于

**结构**：so + 形容词/副词 + that

**例句**：
1. It was **so cold that** the river froze.（天太冷了，以至于河流结冰了。）
2. He ran **so fast that** no one could catch him.（他跑得太快了，以至于没人能追上他。）
3. The movie was **so boring that** I fell asleep.（电影太无聊了，以至于我睡着了。）

#### such...that：如此……以至于

**结构**：such + 名词短语 + that

**例句**：
1. It was **such a cold day that** the river froze.（天太冷了，以至于河流结冰了。）
2. He told **such a funny joke that** everyone laughed.（他讲了一个如此有趣的笑话，以至于每个人都笑了。）
3. She is **such a kind person that** everyone likes her.（她是如此善良的人，以至于每个人都喜欢她。）

#### so vs such

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| so + adj./adv. + that | It was **so cold that**... |
| such + a/an + adj. + n. + that | It was **such a cold day that**... |
| such + adj. + 复数/不可数n. + that | He told **such funny jokes that**... |

**特殊情况**：
- so + many/much/few/little + n. + that
  - There were **so many people that** I couldn't find her.
  - He had **so little money that** he couldn't buy food.

## 三、辨析（Analysis）

### 3.1 中文母语者常见错误

#### 错误1：because和so连用

- ✗ **Because** it was raining, **so** we stayed home.
- ✓ **Because** it was raining, we stayed home.
- ✓ It was raining, **so** we stayed home.

**原因**：because和so不能在同一个句子中连用。

#### 错误2：so...that和such...that混淆

- ✗ It was **such cold that** the river froze.
- ✓ It was **so cold that** the river froze.
- ✓ It was **such a cold day that** the river froze.

**原因**：so后接形容词/副词，such后接名词短语。

#### 错误3：目的状语从句没有情态动词

- ✗ He spoke quietly **so that** no one heard.
- ✓ He spoke quietly **so that** no one **could** hear.

**原因**：目的状语从句通常需要情态动词。

#### 错误4：for放在句首

- ✗ **For** it was raining, we stayed home.
- ✓ We stayed home, **for** it was raining.

**原因**：for是并列连词，不能放在句首。

### 3.2 特殊情况

#### 情况1：because of / due to / owing to

这些是介词短语，后接名词或名词短语，不接从句。

**例句**：
- I stayed at home **because of** the rain.
- The game was cancelled **due to** bad weather.
- **Owing to** his hard work, he passed the exam.

#### 情况2：so that省略that

在口语中，so that可以省略that。

**例句**：
- He spoke quietly **so** no one could hear.（口语中省略that）

## 四、练习（Practice）

### 练习1：原因状语从句填空

选择正确的原因引导词填空。

1. I stayed at home ______ I was sick.
2. ______ you are tired, let's take a break.
3. ______ it was getting dark, we went home.
4. He must be at home, ______ the light is on.

**答案**：
1. because
2. Since
3. As
4. for

### 练习2：so...that / such...that填空

用so...that或such...that完成句子。

1. It was ______ cold ______ the river froze.
2. It was ______ a cold day ______ the river froze.
3. He ran ______ fast ______ no one could catch him.
4. She told ______ a funny joke ______ everyone laughed.
5. There were ______ many people ______ I couldn't find her.

**答案**：
1. so...that
2. such...that
3. so...that
4. such...that
5. so...that

### 练习3：改错

找出并改正以下句子中的错误。

1. Because it was raining, so we stayed home.
2. It was such cold that the river froze.
3. He spoke quietly so that no one heard.
4. For it was late, we went home.

**答案**：
1. **Because** it was raining, we stayed home. / It was raining, **so** we stayed home.
2. It was **so cold** that the river froze. / It was **such a cold day** that the river froze.
3. He spoke quietly so that no one **could** hear.
4. We went home, **for** it was late.

### 练习4：翻译练习

将以下中文句子翻译成英文。

1. 因为下雨，我们待在家里。
2. 她努力学习以便能通过考试。
3. 天太冷了，以至于河流结冰了。
4. 既然你累了，我们休息一下。

**参考答案**：
1. **Because** it was raining, we stayed home.
2. She studied hard **so that** she could pass the exam.
3. It was **so cold that** the river froze.
4. **Since** you are tired, let's take a break.

## 五、产出（Production）

### 任务1：因果关系表达

用because/since/as描述你最近做的一件事的原因。

**示例**：
- I stayed at home yesterday **because** I was sick.
- **Since** the weather was nice, I went for a walk.
- I decided to study English **because** I want to travel abroad.

### 任务2：目的与结果

用so that/so...that/such...that描述你的经历。

**示例**：
- I studied hard **so that** I could pass the exam.
- The movie was **so boring that** I fell asleep.
- It was **such a beautiful day that** we went to the park.

## 六、总结（Summary）

### 核心要点

1. **原因状语从句**：
   - because：语气最强，直接原因
   - since：已知原因
   - as：已知原因
   - for：补充说明（不能放句首）
   - because和so不能连用

2. **目的状语从句**：
   - so that：以便（常用情态动词）
   - in order that：为了（更正式）

3. **结果状语从句**：
   - so + adj./adv. + that
   - such + n. + that
   - so + many/much/few/little + n. + that

### 对比表格

| 类型 | 引导词 | 例句 |
|------|--------|------|
| 原因 | because | I stayed home **because** I was sick. |
| 原因 | since | **Since** you're tired, let's rest. |
| 原因 | as | **As** it was dark, we went home. |
| 原因 | for | He's home, **for** the light is on. |
| 目的 | so that | I'll give you my number **so that** you can call. |
| 结果 | so...that | It was **so cold that** the river froze. |
| 结果 | such...that | It was **such a cold day that** the river froze. |
