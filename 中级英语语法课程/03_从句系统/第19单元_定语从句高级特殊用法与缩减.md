# 第19单元：定语从句高级——特殊用法与缩减

## 学习目标

完成本单元后，你能够：
1. 掌握定语从句的特殊用法
2. 理解定语从句的缩减方法
3. 在写作中灵活运用定语从句

---

## 一、导入（Lead-in）

### 思考题

请看以下句子，思考如何简化：

1. The man **who is standing there** is my friend.
2. The book **which was written by him** is interesting.
3. The house **which was built in 1990** is old.

如何简化这些定语从句？

**答案**：
1. The man **standing there** is my friend.
2. The book **written by him** is interesting.
3. The house **built in 1990** is old.

---

## 二、讲解（Presentation）

### 2.1 定语从句的缩减

定语从句可以缩减为短语，使句子更简洁。

#### 规则1：关系代词作主语 + be动词 → 省略关系代词和be动词

**例句**：
- The man **who is standing there** → The man **standing there**
- The book **which was written by him** → The book **written by him**
- The house **which was built in 1990** → The house **built in 1990**

#### 规则2：关系代词作主语 + 主动动词 → 现在分词

**例句**：
- The man **who stands there** → The man **standing there**
- The students **who study hard** → The students **studying hard**

#### 规则3：关系代词作主语 + 被动动词 → 过去分词

**例句**：
- The book **which was written by him** → The book **written by him**
- The house **which was built in 1990** → The house **built in 1990**

### 2.2 不能缩减的情况

#### 情况1：关系代词作宾语

**例句**：
- The book **that I bought** → 不能缩减为 ~~The book I bought~~（虽然省略了that，但不是分词短语）

#### 情况2：关系副词引导的定语从句

**例句**：
- The city **where I live** → 不能缩减

#### 情况3：非限定性定语从句

**例句**：
- My brother, **who lives in London** → 不能缩减

### 2.3 定语从句的特殊用法

#### 用法1：介词 + 关系代词

**例句**：
- The man **to whom I talked** is my friend.
- The book **in which I found the answer** is old.
- The house **in which he lives** is big.

**注意**：介词可以放在关系代词前或从句末尾。

#### 用法2：名词/代词 + of + 关系代词

**例句**：
- I have two brothers, **both of whom** are doctors.
- There are many books, **some of which** are old.
- He has many friends, **none of whom** I know.

#### 用法3：the way + 定语从句

**例句**：
- I don't like **the way he talks**.（我不喜欢他说话的方式。）
- **The way she sings** is beautiful.（她唱歌的方式很美。）

**注意**：the way后可以用that/in which或省略。

---

## 三、辨析（Analysis）

### 3.1 中文母语者常见错误

#### 错误1：不能缩减的定语从句强行缩减

- ✗ The city **where I live** → ~~The city living~~
- ✓ The city **where I live**（不能缩减）

#### 错误2：介词位置错误

- ✗ The man **whom I talked to him** is my friend.
- ✓ The man **whom I talked to** is my friend.
- ✓ The man **to whom I talked** is my friend.

#### 错误3：of whom/of which用法错误

- ✗ I have two brothers, **both of they** are doctors.
- ✓ I have two brothers, **both of whom** are doctors.

## 四、练习（Practice）

### 练习1：定语从句缩减

将以下定语从句缩减为短语。

1. The man who is standing there is my friend.
2. The book which was written by him is interesting.
3. The students who study hard will pass.
4. The house which was built in 1990 is old.
5. The girl who is singing is my sister.

**答案**：
1. The man **standing there** is my friend.
2. The book **written by him** is interesting.
3. The students **studying hard** will pass.
4. The house **built in 1990** is old.
5. The girl **singing** is my sister.

### 练习2：of whom/of which练习

用of whom/of which完成句子。

1. I have two brothers, both ______ are doctors.
2. There are many books, some ______ are old.
3. He has many friends, none ______ I know.
4. There are 50 students, most ______ are from China.
5. She bought many dresses, a few ______ were expensive.

**答案**：
1. of whom
2. of which
3. of whom
4. of whom
5. of which

### 练习3：改错

找出并改正以下句子中的错误。

1. The city where I live is beautiful. (尝试缩减)
2. The man whom I talked to him is my friend.
3. I have two brothers, both of they are doctors.
4. The way how he talks is strange.
5. The book that I bought interesting.

**答案**：
1. 不能缩减，原句正确。
2. The man **whom I talked to** is my friend. / The man **to whom I talked** is my friend.
3. I have two brothers, **both of whom** are doctors.
4. The way **he talks** is strange. / The way **that he talks** is strange. / The way **in which he talks** is strange.
5. The book **that I bought** is interesting.

---

## 五、总结（Summary）

### 核心要点

1. **定语从句的缩减**：
   - 关系代词 + be动词 → 省略
   - 主动动词 → 现在分词
   - 被动动词 → 过去分词

2. **不能缩减的情况**：
   - 关系代词作宾语
   - 关系副词引导
   - 非限定性定语从句

3. **特殊用法**：
   - 介词 + 关系代词
   - 名词/代词 + of + 关系代词
   - the way + 定语从句

   - C. stood

   - B. written
   - C. wrote

3. I have two brothers, both ______ are doctors.
   - A. of them
   - B. of whom
   - C. of who

   - B. that
   - C. which

   - A. where
