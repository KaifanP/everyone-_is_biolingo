# 第17单元：定语从句入门——限定性定语从句

## 学习目标

完成本单元后，你能够：
1. 理解定语从句的概念和功能
2. 掌握关系代词who/whom/whose/which/that的用法
3. 掌握关系副词where/when/why的用法
4. 在句子中正确使用限定性定语从句

---

## 一、导入（Lead-in）

### 思考题

请看以下句子，思考划线部分的作用：

1. The man **who came** is my friend.
2. The book **that I bought** is interesting.
3. The city **where I live** is beautiful.
4. The time **when we met** was special.

这些划线部分修饰什么？

**答案**：
1. 修饰the man
2. 修饰the book
3. 修饰the city
4. 修饰the time

---

## 二、讲解（Presentation）

### 2.1 定语从句的概念

**定语从句**是修饰名词的从句，相当于形容词的作用。

**例句**：
- The man **is my friend**.（那个人是我的朋友。）
- **Who came yesterday**（昨天来的）
- 合并：The man **who came yesterday** is my friend.（昨天来的那个人是我的朋友。）

### 2.2 关系代词

关系代词引导定语从句，并在从句中充当成分。

| 关系代词 | 指代 | 在从句中作 |
|----------|------|------------|
| who | 人 | 主语/宾语 |
| whom | 人 | 宾语 |
| whose | 人/物 | 定语 |
| which | 物 | 主语/宾语 |
| that | 人/物 | 主语/宾语 |

#### who/that指人作主语

**例句**：
1. The man **who/that came** is my friend.（来的人是我的朋友。）
2. The student **who/that studies hard** will pass.（努力学习的学生会通过。）
3. The girl **who/that is singing** is my sister.（唱歌的女孩是我妹妹。）

#### who/whom/that指人作宾语

**例句**：
1. The man **who/whom/that I met** is my friend.（我遇见的人是我的朋友。）
2. The student **who/whom/that I helped** passed the exam.（我帮助的学生通过了考试。）
3. The girl **who/whom/that you invited** is my sister.（你邀请的女孩是我妹妹。）

**注意**：作宾语时，关系代词可以省略。
- The man **I met** is my friend.

#### whose表示所属

**例句**：
1. The man **whose car is red** is my friend.（车是红色的人是我的朋友。）
2. The student **whose father is a doctor** studies hard.（父亲是医生的学生努力学习。）
3. The book **whose cover is blue** is mine.（封面是蓝色的书是我的。）

#### which/that指物

**例句**：
1. The book **which/that I bought** is interesting.（我买的书很有趣。）
2. The movie **which/that we watched** was great.（我们看的电影很棒。）
3. The house **which/that is near the park** is mine.（公园附近的房子是我的。）

**注意**：作宾语时，关系代词可以省略。
- The book **I bought** is interesting.

### 2.3 关系副词

关系副词引导定语从句，并在从句中作状语。

| 关系副词 | 指代 | 在从句中作 |
|----------|------|------------|
| where | 地点 | 状语 |
| when | 时间 | 状语 |
| why | 原因 | 状语 |

#### where表示地点

**例句**：
1. The city **where I live** is beautiful.（我住的城市很美。）
2. The school **where I studied** is old.（我学习的学校很老。）
3. The house **where he lives** is big.（他住的房子很大。）

**注意**：where = in/at which
- The city **where I live** = The city **in which I live**

#### when表示时间

**例句**：
1. The time **when we met** was special.（我们见面的时间很特别。）
2. The day **when he arrived** was rainy.（他到达的那天下雨。）
3. The year **when I graduated** was 2010.（我毕业的那年是2010年。）

**注意**：when = in/on which
- The time **when we met** = The time **at which we met**

#### why表示原因

**例句**：
1. The reason **why he left** is unknown.（他离开的原因未知。）
2. The reason **why she cried** is clear.（她哭的原因很清楚。）

**注意**：why = for which
- The reason **why he left** = The reason **for which he left**

### 2.4 that vs which

在限定性定语从句中，that和which通常可以互换，但有些情况只能用that。

#### 只用that的情况

1. **先行词是不定代词**：everything, anything, nothing, all, any, little, few等
   - Everything **that he said** is true.
   - Is there anything **that I can do**?

2. **先行词被序数词修饰**
   - The first thing **that I did** was to call him.

3. **先行词被最高级修饰**
   - The best movie **that I've seen** is Inception.

4. **先行词被the only, the very, the same修饰**
   - The only person **that I trust** is my mother.

5. **先行词既有人又有物**
   - The man and his dog **that I saw** were walking in the park.

#### 只用which的情况

1. **非限定性定语从句**（下一单元学习）
2. **介词后**
   - The book **in which I found the answer** is old.

## 三、辨析（Analysis）

### 3.1 中文母语者常见错误

#### 错误1：关系代词和人称代词重复

- ✗ The man **who he came** is my friend.
- ✓ The man **who came** is my friend.

**原因**：关系代词已经代替了人称代词。

#### 错误2：关系副词和介词重复

- ✗ The city **where I live in** is beautiful.
- ✓ The city **where I live** is beautiful.
- ✓ The city **in which I live** is beautiful.

**原因**：where已经包含了介词的含义。

#### 错误3：that和which混用

- ✗ Everything **which he said** is true.
- ✓ Everything **that he said** is true.

**原因**：先行词是不定代词时只能用that。

#### 错误4：关系代词作宾语时不能省略

- ✗ The book **that I bought it** is interesting.
- ✓ The book **that I bought** is interesting.
- ✓ The book **I bought** is interesting.

### 3.2 特殊情况

#### 情况1：定语从句的谓语动词

定语从句的谓语动词与**先行词**一致。

**例句**：
- The student who **studies** hard will pass.（student是单数）
- The students who **study** hard will pass.（students是复数）

#### 情况2：介词提前

介词可以放在关系代词前。

**例句**：
- The man **who/whom I talked to** is my friend.
- The man **to whom I talked** is my friend.（更正式）

- The book **which/that I found the answer in** is old.
- The book **in which I found the answer** is old.（更正式）

## 四、练习（Practice）

### 练习1：关系代词填空

选择正确的关系代词填空。

1. The man ______ came is my friend.
2. The book ______ I bought is interesting.
3. The city ______ I live is beautiful.
4. The reason ______ he left is unknown.
5. The girl ______ father is a doctor studies hard.
6. Everything ______ he said is true.
7. The first thing ______ I did was to call him.
8. The best movie ______ I've seen is Inception.

**答案**：
1. who/that
2. which/that
3. where
4. why
5. whose
6. that
7. that
8. that

### 练习2：句子合并

用定语从句合并两个句子。

**示例**：
- The man is my friend. He came yesterday.
- **The man who came yesterday is my friend.**

1. The book is interesting. I bought it yesterday.
2. The city is beautiful. I live there.
3. The student will pass. He studies hard.
4. The reason is unknown. He left for that reason.

**答案**：
1. The book **that/which I bought yesterday** is interesting.
2. The city **where I live** is beautiful.
3. The student **who/that studies hard** will pass.
4. The reason **why he left** is unknown.

### 练习3：改错

找出并改正以下句子中的错误。

1. The man who he came is my friend.
2. The city where I live in is beautiful.
3. Everything which he said is true.
4. The book that I bought it is interesting.
5. The girl which father is a doctor studies hard.

**答案**：
1. The man **who** came is my friend.
2. The city **where** I live is beautiful.
3. Everything **that** he said is true.
4. The book **that** I bought is interesting.
5. The girl **whose** father is a doctor studies hard.

### 练习4：翻译练习

将以下中文句子翻译成英文。

2. 我买的书很有趣。
3. 我住的城市很美。
4. 他离开的原因未知。
5. 努力学习的学生会通过考试。

**参考答案**：

2. The book **that/which I bought** is interesting.
3. The city **where I live** is beautiful.
4. The reason **why he left** is unknown.

## 五、总结（Summary）

### 核心要点

1. **定语从句**：修饰名词的从句

2. **关系代词**：who, whom, whose, which, that
   - who/whom：指人
   - which：指物
   - that：指人或物
   - whose：表示所属

3. **关系副词**：where, when, why
   - where：地点
   - when：时间
   - why：原因

4. **that vs which**：
   - 不定代词后只能用that
   - 序数词/最高级后只能用that

### 对比表格

| 关系词 | 指代 | 在从句中作 | 例句 |
|--------|------|------------|------|
| who | 人 | 主语/宾语 | The man who came. |
| whom | 人 | 宾语 | The man whom I met. |
| whose | 人/物 | 定语 | The man whose car is red. |
| which | 物 | 主语/宾语 | The book which I bought. |
| that | 人/物 | 主语/宾语 | The book that I bought. |
| where | 地点 | 状语 | The city where I live. |
| when | 时间 | 状语 | The time when we met. |
| why | 原因 | 状语 | The reason why he left. |
