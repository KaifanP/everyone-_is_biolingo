# 第18单元：定语从句进阶——非限定性定语从句

## 学习目标

完成本单元后，你能够：
1. 理解限定性和非限定性定语从句的区别
2. 掌握非限定性定语从句的用法
3. 理解which指代整个句子的用法
4. 在写作中正确使用非限定性定语从句

---

## 一、导入（Lead-in）

### 思考题

请看以下两组句子，思考它们的区别：

**第一组**：
1. The students **who passed the exam** are happy.（通过考试的学生很高兴。）
2. The students, **who passed the exam**, are happy.（学生们很高兴，他们都通过了考试。）

**第二组**：
1. My brother **who lives in London** is a doctor.（我住在伦敦的哥哥是医生。——可能有多个哥哥）
2. My brother, **who lives in London**, is a doctor.（我哥哥是医生，他住在伦敦。——只有一个哥哥）

这两组句子有什么不同？

**答案**：
- 第1句（限定性）：限定先行词，区分"哪些"
- 第2句（非限定性）：补充说明，不区分

---

## 二、讲解（Presentation）

### 2.1 限定性 vs 非限定性定语从句

| | 限定性定语从句 | 非限定性定语从句 |
|------|----------------|------------------|
| **功能** | 限定先行词 | 补充说明 |
| **逗号** | 无逗号 | 有逗号 |
| **能否省略** | 省略后意思不完整 | 省略后意思完整 |
| **that** | 可以用 | 不能用 |

**例句对比**：
- 限定性：The students **who passed** are happy.（限定"哪些学生"）
- 非限定性：The students, **who passed**, are happy.（补充说明"学生们都通过了"）

### 2.2 非限定性定语从句的用法

#### 补充说明先行词

**例句**：
1. My brother, **who lives in London**, is a doctor.（我哥哥是医生，他住在伦敦。）
2. The book, **which I bought yesterday**, is interesting.（这本书很有趣，我昨天买的。）
3. Beijing, **which is the capital of China**, is a big city.（北京是大城市，它是中国的首都。）

#### which指代整个句子

**例句**：
1. He passed the exam, **which surprised everyone**.（他通过了考试，这让每个人都惊讶。）
2. She didn't come, **which made me sad**.（她没来，这让我伤心。）
3. He won the game, **which was unexpected**.（他赢了比赛，这是意想不到的。）

### 2.3 非限定性定语从句的关系词

| 关系词 | 指代 | 例句 |
|--------|------|------|
| who | 人 | My brother, who lives in London. |
| whom | 人（宾语） | My brother, whom I love. |
| whose | 所属 | My brother, whose wife is a teacher. |
| which | 物/句子 | The book, which I bought. |
| where | 地点 | Beijing, where I live. |
| when | 时间 | Last year, when I graduated. |

**注意**：非限定性定语从句**不能用that**。

### 2.4 非限定性定语从句的标点

**重要**：非限定性定语从句必须用逗号隔开。

**例句**：
- ✓ My brother, who lives in London, is a doctor.
- ✗ My brother who lives in London is a doctor.（限定性）

---

## 三、辨析（Analysis）

### 3.1 中文母语者常见错误

#### 错误1：非限定性定语从句用that

- ✗ My brother, **that lives in London**, is a doctor.
- ✓ My brother, **who lives in London**, is a doctor.

#### 错误2：缺少逗号

- ✗ My brother who lives in London is a doctor.（如果想表达非限定性）
- ✓ My brother, who lives in London, is a doctor.

#### 错误3：which指代不清

- ✗ He passed the exam, which was good.（which指代不清）
- ✓ He passed the exam, which surprised everyone.（which指代整个句子）

### 3.2 特殊情况

#### 情况1：非限定性定语从句的省略

非限定性定语从句可以省略，不影响主句意思。

**例句**：
- My brother, who lives in London, is a doctor.
- My brother is a doctor.（省略后意思完整）

#### 情况2：介词+which

**例句**：
- The book, **in which I found the answer**, is old.
- My brother, **with whom I live**, is a doctor.

## 四、练习（Practice）

### 练习1：限定性 vs 非限定性判断

判断以下句子是限定性还是非限定性定语从句。

1. The students who passed are happy.
2. The students, who passed, are happy.
3. My brother who lives in London is a doctor.
4. My brother, who lives in London, is a doctor.
5. The book which I bought is interesting.
6. The book, which I bought yesterday, is interesting.

**答案**：
1. 限定性
2. 非限定性
3. 限定性
4. 非限定性
5. 限定性
6. 非限定性

### 练习2：非限定性定语从句填空

用正确的关系词填空。

1. My brother, ______ lives in London, is a doctor.
2. The book, ______ I bought yesterday, is interesting.
3. He passed the exam, ______ surprised everyone.
4. Beijing, ______ is the capital of China, is a big city.
5. Last year, ______ I graduated, was special.

**答案**：
1. who
2. which
3. which
4. which
5. when

### 练习3：改错

找出并改正以下句子中的错误。

1. My brother, that lives in London, is a doctor.
2. The students who passed, are happy.
3. He passed the exam that surprised everyone.
4. The book, which I bought it, is interesting.
5. My brother who lives in London, is a doctor.

**答案**：
1. My brother, **who** lives in London, is a doctor.
2. The students**,** who passed**,** are happy. (加逗号)
3. He passed the exam**,** which surprised everyone. (用逗号，非限定性)
4. The book, which I bought, is interesting. (去掉it)
5. My brother**,** who lives in London**,** is a doctor. (加逗号)

### 练习4：句子合并

用非限定性定语从句合并两个句子。

**示例**：
- My brother is a doctor. He lives in London.
- **My brother, who lives in London, is a doctor.**

1. The book is interesting. I bought it yesterday.
2. He passed the exam. This surprised everyone.
3. Beijing is a big city. It is the capital of China.

**答案**：
1. The book, **which I bought yesterday**, is interesting.
2. He passed the exam, **which surprised everyone**.
3. Beijing, **which is the capital of China**, is a big city.

---

## 五、总结（Summary）

### 核心要点

1. **限定性定语从句**：限定先行词，无逗号
2. **非限定性定语从句**：补充说明，有逗号
3. **非限定性不能用that**
4. **which可以指代整个句子**

### 对比表格

| | 限定性 | 非限定性 |
|------|--------|----------|
| 逗号 | 无 | 有 |
| that | 可用 | 不可用 |
| 功能 | 限定 | 补充 |
| 省略 | 意思不完整 | 意思完整 |
