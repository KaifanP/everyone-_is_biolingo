# 第24单元：从句综合与实战

## 学习目标

完成本单元后，你能够：
1. 综合运用名词性从句、定语从句和状语从句
2. 在复杂语境中选择正确的从句类型
3. 在写作中灵活运用各种从句
4. 在考试中准确完成从句相关题目

## 一、导入（Lead-in）

### 思考题

请看以下句子，判断划线部分属于哪种从句：

1. I know **that he is right**.
2. The man **who came** is my friend.
3. I'll go **if he goes**.
4. **What he said** surprised me.
5. The city **where I live** is beautiful.
6. **Although he was tired**, he kept working.

**答案**：
1. 名词性从句（宾语从句）
2. 定语从句（限定性）
3. 状语从句（条件）
4. 名词性从句（主语从句）
5. 定语从句（限定性）
6. 状语从句（让步）

## 二、讲解（Presentation）

### 2.1 三大从句综合对比

| 从句类型 | 功能 | 引导词 | 例句 |
|----------|------|--------|------|
| 名词性从句 | 作名词 | that/whether/what/who等 | I know **that he is right**. |
| 定语从句 | 作形容词 | who/which/that/where等 | The man **who came** is my friend. |
| 状语从句 | 作副词 | when/if/because/although等 | I'll go **if he goes**. |

### 2.2 从句选择策略

#### 第一步：判断从句在句子中的作用

- 作名词（主语、宾语、表语、同位语）→ 名词性从句
- 修饰名词 → 定语从句
- 修饰动词/句子（时间、地点、原因、条件等）→ 状语从句

#### 第二步：选择正确的引导词

**名词性从句引导词**：
| 引导词 | 含义 | 用法 |
|--------|------|------|
| that | （无意义） | 陈述句 |
| whether | 是否 | 疑问句 |
| what | 什么 | 在从句中作成分 |
| who | 谁 | 在从句中作成分 |
| where | 哪里 | 在从句中作状语 |
| when | 什么时候 | 在从句中作状语 |
| why | 为什么 | 在从句中作状语 |
| how | 怎样 | 在从句中作状语 |

**定语从句引导词**：
| 引导词 | 指代 | 在从句中作 |
|--------|------|------------|
| who/whom | 人 | 主语/宾语 |
| which | 物 | 主语/宾语 |
| that | 人/物 | 主语/宾语 |
| whose | 人/物 | 定语 |
| where | 地点 | 状语 |
| when | 时间 | 状语 |
| why | 原因 | 状语 |

**状语从句引导词**：
| 类型 | 引导词 |
|------|--------|
| 时间 | when/while/as/before/after/until/since/as soon as |
| 地点 | where/wherever |
| 原因 | because/since/as/for |
| 目的 | so that/in order that |
| 结果 | so...that/such...that |
| 条件 | if/unless/provided that/as long as |
| 让步 | although/even though/while/however/whatever |
| 方式 | as/as if/as though |
| 比较 | than/as...as |

### 2.3 从句综合运用

#### 复合句中的多层从句

一个句子中可以包含多个从句。

**例句**：
1. I know **that** the man **who came** is my friend.
   - that引导宾语从句
   - who引导定语从句（修饰the man）

2. **Although** he was tired, he told me **that** he would come.
   - although引导让步状语从句
   - that引导宾语从句

3. The book **that** I bought **when** I was in London is interesting.
   - that引导定语从句（修饰the book）
   - when引导时间状语从句

4. **What** he said **when** he heard the news surprised everyone.
   - what引导主语从句
   - when引导时间状语从句

#### 从句嵌套的层次分析

**例句**：I believe **that** the fact **that** he lied **when** he was questioned is true.

分析：
1. I believe **that...** → 宾语从句
2. the fact **that he lied...** → 同位语从句（解释the fact）
3. **when he was questioned** → 时间状语从句（修饰lied）

### 2.4 从句在写作中的应用

#### 写作中的从句功能

| 从句类型 | 写作功能 | 示例 |
|----------|----------|------|
| 名词性从句 | 表达观点、事实 | I believe **that** education is important. |
| 定语从句 | 提供细节、描述 | The book, **which I bought yesterday**, is interesting. |
| 状语从句 | 表达逻辑关系 | **Although** it was raining, we had fun. |

#### 写作中的从句组合

**示例段落**：
> I believe **that** learning English is important **because** it opens doors to many opportunities. The book **that** I'm reading, **which** was recommended by my teacher, has helped me understand grammar better. **Although** it's difficult, I'll keep studying **until** I become fluent.

分析：
- that learning English is important → 宾语从句
- because it opens doors... → 原因状语从句
- that I'm reading → 定语从句（修饰the book）
- which was recommended... → 非限定性定语从句（补充说明）
- Although it's difficult → 让步状语从句
- until I become fluent → 时间状语从句

### 2.5 从句在考试中的常见考点

#### 考点1：引导词选择

**题目**：I don't know ______ he will come.
- A. that
- B. whether
- C. what

**答案**：B（whether表示"是否"）

#### 考点2：从句语序

**题目**：I don't know ______.
- A. where does he live
- B. where he does live
- C. where he lives

**答案**：C（名词性从句用陈述语序）

#### 考点3：that vs which

**题目**：Everything ______ he said is true.
- A. which
- B. that
- C. what

**答案**：B（不定代词后只能用that）

#### 考点4：非限定性定语从句

**题目**：My brother, ______ lives in London, is a doctor.
- A. that
- B. who
- C. which

**答案**：B（非限定性定语从句不能用that）

#### 考点5：时态一致

**题目**：I'll call you when I ______ home.
- A. will arrive
- B. arrive
- C. arrived

**答案**：B（时间状语从句用一般现在时代替将来时）

## 三、辨析（Analysis）

### 3.1 中文母语者常见错误

#### 错误1：从句类型混淆

- ✗ I know **what** he is right.（想说"我知道他是对的"）
- ✓ I know **that** he is right.

**原因**：what在从句中作成分，that不作成分。"他是对的"是完整句子，不需要what。

#### 错误2：定语从句和状语从句混淆

- ✗ The reason **because** he left is unknown.
- ✓ The reason **why** he left is unknown.

**原因**：reason后用why引导定语从句，不用because。

#### 错误3：从句语序错误

- ✗ I don't know **where does he live**.
- ✓ I don't know **where he lives**.

**原因**：名词性从句用陈述语序。

#### 错误4：引导词缺失

- ✗ I know he is right.
- ✓ I know **that** he is right.

**原因**：正式写作中that通常不省略。

### 3.2 特殊情况

#### 情况1：that的多重角色

that可以引导：
1. 名词性从句（宾语从句）：I know **that** he is right.
2. 定语从句：The man **that** came is my friend.
3. 状语从句（so...that）：It was **so** cold **that** the river froze.

**区分方法**：看that在从句中是否作成分。
- 名词性从句：that不作成分
- 定语从句：that作成分（主语/宾语）

#### 情况2：what的特殊性

what = the thing that，在从句中作成分。

**例句**：
- **What he said** surprised me. = **The thing that he said** surprised me.

## 四、练习（Practice）

### 练习1：从句类型判断

判断以下句子中从句的类型。

1. I know that he is right.
2. The man who came is my friend.
3. I'll go if he goes.
4. What he said surprised me.
5. The city where I live is beautiful.
6. Although he was tired, he kept working.
7. The news that he won made us happy.
8. I don't know whether he will come.

**答案**：
1. 名词性从句（宾语从句）
2. 定语从句（限定性）
3. 状语从句（条件）
4. 名词性从句（主语从句）
5. 定语从句（限定性）
6. 状语从句（让步）
7. 名词性从句（同位语从句）
8. 名词性从句（宾语从句）

### 练习2：引导词选择

选择正确的引导词填空。

1. I don't know ______ he will come.
2. The man ______ came is my friend.
3. I'll go ______ he goes.
4. ______ he said surprised me.
5. The city ______ I live is beautiful.
6. ______ he was tired, he kept working.
7. The news ______ he won made us happy.
8. It was ______ cold ______ the river froze.

**答案**：
1. whether
2. who/that
3. if
4. What
5. where
6. Although
7. that
8. so...that

### 练习3：复合句分析

分析以下句子中的从句层次。

1. I know that the man who came is my friend.
2. Although he was tired, he told me that he would come.
3. The book that I bought when I was in London is interesting.
4. What he said when he heard the news surprised everyone.

**答案**：
1. I know **that the man who came is my friend**.
   - that引导宾语从句
   - who引导定语从句（修饰the man）

2. **Although he was tired**, he told me **that he would come**.
   - although引导让步状语从句
   - that引导宾语从句

3. **The book that I bought when I was in London** is interesting.
   - that引导定语从句（修饰the book）
   - when引导时间状语从句

4. **What he said when he heard the news** surprised everyone.
   - what引导主语从句
   - when引导时间状语从句

### 练习4：综合练习

用从句完成句子。

1. 我知道他是对的。→ I know ______ he is right.
2. 昨天来的那个人是我的朋友。→ The man ______ came yesterday is my friend.
3. 如果下雨，我们就待在家里。→ ______ it rains, we'll stay at home.
4. 他说的话让我惊讶。→ ______ he said surprised me.
5. 虽然他很累，但他继续工作。→ ______ he was tired, he kept working.

**答案**：
1. **that**
2. **who/that**
3. **If**
4. **What**
5. **Although**

## 六、总结（Summary）

### 核心要点

1. **三大从句类型**：
   - 名词性从句：作名词（主语、宾语、表语、同位语）
   - 定语从句：作形容词（修饰名词）
   - 状语从句：作副词（修饰动词、句子）

2. **从句选择策略**：
   - 第一步：判断从句在句子中的作用
   - 第二步：选择正确的引导词

3. **常见考点**：
   - 引导词选择
   - 从句语序（陈述语序）
   - that vs which
   - 非限定性定语从句
   - 时态一致

4. **写作应用**：
   - 名词性从句：表达观点、事实
   - 定语从句：提供细节、描述
   - 状语从句：表达逻辑关系

### 综合对比表

| 从句类型 | 功能 | 引导词 | 考点 |
|----------|------|--------|------|
| 名词性从句 | 作名词 | that/whether/what等 | 语序、that省略 |
| 定语从句 | 作形容词 | who/which/that等 | that vs which、限定/非限定 |
| 状语从句 | 作副词 | when/if/because等 | 时态、although和but |
