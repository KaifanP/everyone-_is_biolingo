# 第41单元 连词与句子连接

## 学习目标
1. 掌握并列连词的用法
2. 掌握从属连词的用法
3. 理解连词在句子连接中的作用
4. 能够正确使用连词写复合句

## 一、导入 (Lead-in)

### 思考问题
- 并列连词和从属连词有什么区别？
- 如何用连词使文章更连贯？

### 对比观察
- **并列连词：** I like tea **and** he likes coffee.
- **从属连词：** I stayed at home **because** it was raining.

## 二、讲解 (Presentation)

### 2.1 并列连词

**连接两个对等的成分**

| 连词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| and | 和 | I like tea and coffee. |
| but | 但是 | I like tea, but he likes coffee. |
| or | 或者 | Do you like tea or coffee? |
| so | 所以 | It was raining, so I stayed at home. |
| yet | 然而 | He is rich, yet he is not happy. |
| for | 因为 | He must be ill, for he looks pale. |

**注意：** 并列连词连接两个完整的句子

### 2.2 从属连词

**引导从句，连接主句和从句**

**1. 时间从属连词**
| 连词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| when | 当…时 | When I arrived, he was sleeping. |
| while | 当…时（持续） | While I was reading, he was cooking. |
| before | 在…之前 | Wash your hands before you eat. |
| after | 在…之后 | After I finished, I went home. |
| until | 直到 | Wait until I come back. |
| since | 自从 | I have lived here since 2010. |
| as soon as | 一…就 | Call me as soon as you arrive. |

**2. 原因从属连词**
| 连词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| because | 因为 | I stayed home because it was raining. |
| since | 既然 | Since you are tired, go to bed. |
| as | 由于 | As it was late, we went home. |

**3. 条件从属连词**
| 连词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| if | 如果 | If it rains, I will stay home. |
| unless | 除非 | Unless you hurry, you will be late. |
| as long as | 只要 | As long as you try, you will succeed. |

**4. 让步从属连词**
| 连词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| although/though | 虽然 | Although he is young, he is smart. |
| even though | 即使 | Even though it was cold, we went out. |

**5. 目的/结果从属连词**
| 连词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| so that | 以便 | I studied hard so that I could pass. |
| so...that | 如此…以至于 | He was so tired that he fell asleep. |
| such...that | 如此…以至于 | It was such a good movie that I watched it twice. |

### 2.3 连词的使用技巧

**1. 并列连词连接对等成分**
- I like tea and coffee.（名词+名词）
- He is smart and hardworking.（形容词+形容词）
- I came and saw him.（动词+动词）

**2. 从属连词引导从句**
- 从句可以在主句前或主句后
- 从句在前时，后面加逗号

## 三、辨析 (Analysis)

### 常见错误

1. ❌ Although he is young, but he is smart.
   ✅ Although he is young, he is smart.
   ✅ He is young, but he is smart.
   **分析：** although和but不能同时使用

2. ❌ Because it was raining, so I stayed home.
   ✅ Because it was raining, I stayed home.
   ✅ It was raining, so I stayed home.
   **分析：** because和so不能同时使用

3. ❌ I will go if it will not rain.
   ✅ I will go if it does not rain.
   **分析：** 条件从句用一般现在时表将来

### 关键辨析

**because vs since vs as**
| 连词 | 语气 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| because | 最强 | 直接原因 |
| since | 较弱 | 已知原因 |
| as | 最弱 | 显然原因 |

## 四、练习 (Practice)

### 练习1：选择正确的连词
1. I stayed at home ______ (because/so) it was raining.
2. ______ (Although/But) he is young, he is smart.
3. I will go ______ (if/unless) it rains.

### 练习2：用连词连接句子
1. I like tea. He likes coffee. →
2. He is young. He is smart. →
3. It was raining. I stayed at home. →

### 练习3：改正错误
1. Although he is young, but he is smart.
2. Because it was raining, so I stayed home.
3. I will go if it will not rain.

### 任务1：写复合句
用从属连词写5个复合句。

### 任务2：段落写作
用并列连词和从属连词写一段短文（5-6句），主题自选。

## 六、总结 (Summary)

### 关键要点
1. 并列连词：and, but, or, so, yet, for
2. 从属连词：when, while, before, after, until, since, because, if, although等
3. although和but、because和so不能同时使用
4. 条件从句用一般现在时表将来

### 自测题
1. ______ he is young, he is smart.
   A. Although B. But C. Although/But D. Both A and B

2. I stayed home ______ it was raining.
   A. because B. so C. because/so D. Both A and B

3. I will go if it ______ not rain.
   A. will B. does C. is D. was

**答案：1.A 2.A 3.B**
