# 第40单元 介词与介词搭配

## 学习目标
1. 掌握常用介词的基本用法
2. 理解介词与动词、形容词、名词的搭配
3. 掌握常见介词短语
4. 能够正确使用介词搭配

## 一、导入 (Lead-in)

### 思考问题
- 为什么英语中介词的用法如此复杂？
- 如何记忆大量的介词搭配？

### 对比观察
- interested **in**（对…感兴趣）
- good **at**（擅长…）
- depend **on**（依赖于…）
- look forward **to**（期待…）

## 二、讲解 (Presentation)

### 2.1 常用介词的基本含义

| 介词 | 基本含义 | 例句 |
|------|---------|------|
| in | 在…里面/时间 | in the room, in 2020 |
| on | 在…上面/时间 | on the table, on Monday |
| at | 在…地点/时间 | at school, at 3 o'clock |
| by | 通过/到…时 | by bus, by then |
| with | 和/用 | with friends, with a pen |
| for | 为了/持续 | for you, for two hours |
| from | 从… | from China, from now on |
| to | 到/向 | to school, to the park |
| about | 关于 | about the movie |
| of | …的 | the door of the room |

### 2.2 动词 + 介词搭配

**常见搭配：**
| 动词 + 介词 | 例句 |
|------------|------|
| look at | Look at the blackboard. |
| listen to | Listen to the music. |
| wait for | Wait for me. |
| depend on | It depends on the weather. |
| believe in | I believe in you. |
| look forward to | I look forward to seeing you. |
| pay for | I paid for the book. |
| apply for | I applied for the job. |

### 2.3 形容词 + 介词搭配

**常见搭配：**
| 形容词 + 介词 | 例句 |
|--------------|------|
| interested in | I am interested in music. |
| good at | She is good at singing. |
| afraid of | He is afraid of dogs. |
| proud of | I am proud of you. |
| different from | This is different from that. |
| responsible for | He is responsible for the project. |
| familiar with | I am familiar with this place. |
| tired of | I am tired of waiting. |

### 2.4 名词 + 介词搭配

**常见搭配：**
| 名词 + 介词 | 例句 |
|------------|------|
| reason for | the reason for the delay |
| solution to | the solution to the problem |
| interest in | interest in music |
| experience in | experience in teaching |
| difficulty in | difficulty in learning |
| advantage of | the advantage of this method |

## 三、辨析 (Analysis)

### 常见错误

1. ❌ I am interested on music.
   ✅ I am interested in music.
   **分析：** interested后面接in

2. ❌ I am good in English.
   ✅ I am good at English.
   **分析：** good后面接at

3. ❌ I look forward to see you.
   ✅ I look forward to seeing you.
   **分析：** look forward to中的to是介词，后面接doing

### 关键辨析

**at/in/on 时间用法**
| 介词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| at | 具体时刻 | at 3 o'clock |
| in | 月/年/季节/较长时间 | in 2020, in the morning |
| on | 具体日期/星期 | on Monday, on May 1st |

## 四、练习 (Practice)

### 练习1：选择正确的介词
1. I am interested ______ (in/at/on) music.
2. She is good ______ (in/at/on) singing.
3. He is afraid ______ (of/at/in) dogs.

### 练习2：完成介词搭配
1. look ______ the blackboard
2. listen ______ the music
3. wait ______ me
4. depend ______ the weather

### 练习3：改正错误
1. I am good in English.
2. He is interested on music.
3. I look forward to see you.

## 五、产出 (Production)

### 任务1：自我介绍
用至少5个介词搭配写一段自我介绍。
例：I am interested in music. I am good at playing the piano.

### 任务2：描述你的城市
用至少5个介词搭配描述你所在的城市。

## 六、总结 (Summary)

### 关键要点
1. 介词与动词、形容词、名词有固定搭配
2. 常见搭配：look at, listen to, interested in, good at
3. 介词后面接名词或动名词
4. 注意区分at/in/on的时间用法

### 自测题
1. I am interested ______ music.
   A. in B. at C. on D. for

2. She is good ______ singing.
   A. in B. at C. on D. for

3. I look forward to ______ you.
   A. see B. seeing C. seen D. saw

**答案：1.A 2.B 3.B**
